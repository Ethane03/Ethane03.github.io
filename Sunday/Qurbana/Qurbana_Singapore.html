Holy Qurbana

Published by the Mar Thoma Church of Singapore

The Ante-communion

[Eqba]

P:

O Lord Jesus Christ, incarnate of Mary and baptised by John, shower Your blessings on us.

[Maniso of Mar Severus]

C:

O Lord our King, the Word and the only begotten Son of the heavenly Father, we praise You. O Lord our Messiah, though You are immortal in nature, in Your grace, You became incarnate of the blessed Virgin Mary, for the life and salvation of all humanity. Without change of nature, You became the Son of Man, were crucified for us, trampled our death by Your death and destroyed it forever. O Lord our Messiah, who is One in the Holy Trinity, and is worshipped and glorified equally with Your Father and Your living and Holy Spirit, have mercy on us all. Amen.

Husaya

P:

O Lord God, who alone can justify, who alone can make holy, who alone can pardon and completely remove our trespasses, in Your loving compassion, blot out my many great and innumerable sins and those of all Your faithful people. Good Lord, have mercy on us and bless us.

O Lord God, in Your mercy, remember us and our families, our bishops and clergy and all the faithful children of Your holy and glorious Church. O Lord God, comfort our bodies, souls and spirits. Shower on us, the dew of Your mercy and compassion.

O glorious Lord, our Lord of lords, our King and Messiah, absolve us from our sins and be our Redeemer. Answer us O Lord, and rescue us with Your help and support. O God, accept our prayers and petitions, and in Your mercy, save us from harsh punishments and the rod of anger.

Make us all worthy of the blessed end that is reserved for the children of peace. Generously grant us the fullness of Christian perfection that You cherish, is pleasing to You, and befits Your divinity. We offer to You praise and thanksgiving, all the days of our lives, now and forever more.

C:

Amen.

Sedra

P:

Almighty Warrior, Omnipotent and glorious Lord God, with Your power and Your mighty arm, rescue us from the evil one and his hosts. O Lord, in Your grace, mercy and love for humanity, You took flesh of the blessed Virgin Mary and became incarnate for our sake. O Lord of lords, do not banish us from Your presence in Your heavenly abode, where Your greatness is extolled by the four-faced beings who are tied to Your chariots, the company of the angels and archangels who praise Your divinity, the array of cherubim who bless, praise and glorify Your power, the six-winged seraphim who celebrate and proclaim aloud Your holiness and say, “Holy, Holy, Holy, is the Lord God Almighty”, and all the hosts, ranks and companies who worship Your awesome unity of substance with Your Father.

Show us the way of life and salvation, so that we may persevere and attain Your glorious kingdom. O Lord, we thank You for Your graciousness, and pray and plead for Your compassion. O Lord Jesus Christ, have mercy on us and help us. Lord Jesus Christ, look upon us with the eye of Your mercy.

Lord Jesus Christ, save us from our enemies and keep us under the shadow of Your cross. Lord Jesus Christ, deliver us from all the deceits of the evil one, prevent us from falling into the degradation of sin, and lift us from the pits of danger. Lord Jesus Christ, save us from all evil thoughts, impurity and blasphemy. Wash away all loathsome filth and stains, and wipe away all evil and wickedness from us.

Lord Jesus Christ, satisfy us with Your goodness and blessings, and enrich us from the treasury of Your bountiful blessings and mercy. Lord Jesus Christ, allow us to rejoice in Your joyful bridal chamber. Make us rejoice with all the servants and guests who are the children of Your kingdom. Lord Jesus Christ, invite us also, with all the righteous and the saints who are Your beloved. Keep us in the ranks of Your children who are the lambs on Your right. Lord Jesus Christ, on the day when Your magnificence is revealed, make us radiant together with the saints and the chosen ones, and give us a place on Your right at that divine banquet. We will always ascribe praise and thanksgiving to You, to the Father and to the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

To You, to Your only Son and to Your Holy Spirit who is most holy, supreme, adorable and One in essence with You, we ascribe praise and thanksgiving, now and forever more.

Amen

May the love and peace of our Lord Jesus Christ abide with us forever.*

(*Repeat until everyone has received the Kiss of Peace.)

D:

Brothers and sisters, having received this divine peace, let us bow our heads before the merciful Lord.

C:

O gracious Lord our God, we bow our heads in Your holy presence.

P:

O merciful Lord, You dwell on high, yet condescend to look upon things that are lowly. Send Your blessings now, on those who have bowed their heads in Your presence. Bless them with the grace of Your only Son, with whom and with Your Holy Spirit, You are worthy of praise, honour and dominion, all the days of our lives, now and forever more.

C:

Amen.

P:

O God our Father, who out of Your great love for humanity, sent Your Son to the world to rescue the lambs that have gone astray, do not reject this sacrifice of grace, peace and thanksgiving. Depending only on Your mercy and not on our own righteousness, we pray that the sacrifice that You fulfilled for our salvation, not be for our condemnation but for the redemption of our sins. We express our gratefulness to You, Your only Son and to the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

C:

Amen.

(The celebrant removes the veil covering the paten and chalice and prays silently.)

D:

Brothers and sisters, standing respectfully in awe and reverence, with purity, holiness, love, true faith and devotion, let us all participate in this Holy Qurbana which is now being offered. To God the Father, to whom all things belong, is offered this sacrifice of grace, peace and thanksgiving, in a spirit of unity and concord.
