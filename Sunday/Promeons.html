Sunday Liturgy

Promeon and Sedra, to be recited after the main Promeon and Sedra of the Qurbana

Promeon

Praiseworthy is the sweet fruit, and blessed is the beautiful offering who was offered up for us, who gave us the remission of sins, and who cleansed us by his purifying sacrifice. The lamb, the child of the virgin ewe, ascended the cross and rescued us, saved us from the sacrifices of dumb animals, and to him belongs praise and honor and glory, at this time of the divine sacrifice of worship, on all holy days, in all times and hours, and all the days of our lives.

Sedra

O Lord God, the omnipotent, who accepts this sacrifice of praise from those who are calling on you with all their hearts. Accept this incense from us, your sinful servants. Bring us closer to your holy altar. Give us strength to offer unto you spiritual sacrifices and offerings for our sins and for the crimes of your people. Let us act as acceptable sacrifices to you. Let the High Holy Spirit dwell on us, on this offered Qurbana, and on your faithful people, because of our Lord Jesus Messiah. Promeon

To the Lord Messiah, who offered himself as a sacrifice for our deliverance, who broke his body and fed us, who opened his side and gave us drink, and raised us with him into heaven, belongs praise and honor and glory, at this time of the divine sacrifice of worship, on all holy days, in all times, and hours, and all the days of our lives.

Sedra

O God of gods, Lord of lords, Judge of judges, terrible unto the authorities, In whose presence the burning ones tremble, and in whose sight the spirits shake, we worship in your presence, and we pray to your divinity. You have made us worthy to serve you like the holy seraphim, and you have communed with us in the venerable mysteries of your divinity. (O Lord), absolve us by your multitude of mercies, to stand in holiness in your presence and before your holy altar, and serve you in purity aat your holy table. May you sanctify this bread and wine placed before us. Cleanse our bodies and souls. Wipe us from all the mud of sin. May we offer pleasant and spotless sacrifices to you, and make us worthy of the blessings thaa you have promised to the saints. We will gather with them and among them, and bear praise and thanksgiving to you, your father, and holy spirit, hosho...
