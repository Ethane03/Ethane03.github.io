Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord, we honor with pure faith and glorious works the memory of the holy martyr Mar Stephanos Sahda, and we rejoice on this holy day as we celebrate the memory of your beloved one. Therein, our Lord and our God, make us worthy with him to gain crowns of victory and to merit the reward of the workers of good.

C:

Amen.

Psalm 51

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

Merciful Lord...

P:

May we be made worthy...

To the Most High, who taught his soldiers to become martyrs as they descended to battle for him, who strengthened his holy witnesses in the war against the Evil one, who crowned them with crowns of victory, who bequeathed to them the kingdom of heaven, who made as his beloved brother Stephen, the courageous firstborn of the martyrs, mighty and valorant in war, to him belongs praise and honor and worship, at this hour, on all holy days, seasons, and times, and all the days of our lives. Hosho...

C:

Amen.

Sedra

P:

O Lord Messiah, who gathered Stephen, the firstborn of the martyrs, cheif of the deacons, mighty champion, warrior, decorated with divine adornment, the blessed rock, the first sacrifice of the new Gospel, fully set apart as an offering to the Lord, from among the suffering of the glorious crucifixion, who kept the first blessed fruit of the tree of crucifixion safely in the hall of eternal life, who raised him from the world in the beginning as firstfruits unto the alter on high, who made him a sure sacrifice of peace upon the altar of the stones cast by a cursed people, because your servant stephen pleased God your father, he becaume worthy of the odour of spices, like a sweet and ripe cluster of grapes in your vineyard. You gladdened men and angels by it's delightful taste. O Lord Messiah, when you crowned St. Stephen - he who is the priestly grapevine full of spiritual medicine on the everlasting, glorious, and eternal grapestem, shooting bamboo of divine grace, majestic blossom shining with the worthyness of true faith, fragrant lily [Susanna] sprouting with glory in the garden of Golgotha, redolent rose of insatiable love standing unwilting in the inner Paradise, unfading bright flower of hope that we will not be put to shame, unwithering bright green shoot, everlasting bone which pour out immortal honey, the unconquerable city-gates, the rock of unshaking forgiveness [earth?], the mighty soldier who showed dispassion against those who killed God, the great warrior who courageously bore the cross, the revealer of the mystery of the crucifixion, the faithful steward of blessed apostleship, the wonderous man who was like unto a clean censer spreading the incense of your sufferings over the earth - the heavens opened and in the hour of his death you made him like you, for he prayed for those who had come to kill him. O Lord, make haste to save us from the famous and troublesome stoning of the Evil one [Akel Qarusa] and from every decietful punishment of demons, Guard us from the speeding arrows of wicked men and of the Evil one, the enemy, and strengthen us against famous and well-known enemies. Deliver us from every kind of poision of that famous dragon. May we be able to delight at all times in the sweetness of a fortified faith. We will bear praise and thanksgiving to you, your father, and holy spirit, hosho...

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

On those day of the remembrance of Stephen, the firstling of the great ones, upon whom stones were cast like rain, the first to suffer in battle, the elect offering to the King of kings, the acceptable firstfruit, whose frangrance has spread like spices over the whole face of the earth, like a pure fragrance, and like the incense of spices, O Lord our Messiah, may this prayer that we submit before your throne be accepted by you. May our petitions recieve answer from you. Grant us forgiveness of crimes and absolution of sins, which are the fragrance of spices of divine gifts, and the light of majestic and supernal gifts. We shall bear praise and thanksgiving to you, your father, and holy spirit, hosho...

C:

Amen
