Opening Prayer

P:

Make us worthy, Lord God, to celebrate the day of the memorial of your holy Apostles, who were annointed by the Holy Spirit, preached your Gospel throughout all the earth, and bore your holy name before gentiles, kings, and the children of Israel. And make us too worthy of the blessedness promised to them in your heavenly kingdom. With them, among them, and like them, may we lift up praise and thanskgiving to you, your Father, and your Holy Spirit on the day of your glorious appearance.

C:

Amen.

Ethra

P:

Recieve, O Lord, by your exceeding love of mankind, this pure incense that we submit to you on this holy feast of your Apostles. Make us hearers of their divine preaching, make us resemble them in their zeal and fear of God. On that great day, when you come in the glory of your Father, with the holy angels, to reward each person according to their works, and when you prepare the twelve thrones to judge the twelve tribes of Israel, our Lord and our God forever, grant grace to us and all the children of the Church, to find refuge beneath your wings, to join all your holy ones, and to be arranged in their ranks, hosho...

C:

Amen
