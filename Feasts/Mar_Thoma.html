Opening Prayer

P:

Make us worthy, Lord God, to delight in the memory of your beloved, and to celebrate with pure faith and good works this memorial of St. Thomas, who saw your side which had been crucified and pierced by proud men, and thereby became a store-keeper of heavenly gifts, who lit the lamp of true faith [which shines] at all times in the four corners of the world and enlightened creation, and bore afflication and persecution for it, and left his native land and tribe to proclaim it in the world, came to India, and preaced about his saviour. O Lord, let your Holy Spirit overshadow us as He did St. Thomas, and on the last day, may we worship the abundnce of your blessings, and adore and glorify the riches of your mercy.

C:

Amen.

Promeon

P:

C:

Merciful Lord...

P:

May we be made worthy...

You are the one who chose the holy Apostle Mar Thoma as Your disciple, empowered him to preach Your gospel, revealed and gave him Your divine mysteries, confirmed him in the true faith until the last breath, sanctified him from all unholiness, made him an adornment and crown of Your holy church, ordained him as a keeper of your treasure-house, crowned him as the apostle to India, established your life-giving cross as armor on him, and made him worthy to sing praise to you with the heavenly hosts above. And to you belong praise, honor, and glory, now at the celebration of the memory of St. Thomas the apostle, on all...

C:

Amen.

Sedra

P:

Creator of all creatures, God of the hosts above, King of kings, Lord of lords, whose soveriegnty is everlasting, who glorified the ranks of his saints in the heights, who magnifies the authority of those who suffer for His sake, who increases the glory of His righteous ones, who multiplies the joy of His worshippers, who adorns His holy church with His holy gifts and exalts her, who has elevated Her by the Prophets and the Apostles who preached truth, and who glorified His servant Mar Thoma the Apostle as a warrior who won victory among the unwise, O Lord Messiah, thou art worshipped along with your Father and Holy Spirit. Lord God, may you help us by your victorious cross, absolve and forgive our guilt and iniquity, grant us the riches of your divine gifts, and remember your Holy Church. Unceasingly increase eternal peace in your church. Grant watchfulness to her blessed shepherds, diligence to her governers, truth to the judges, acceptable repentance to sinners. May we bear praise and thanksgiving to You, Your Father, and Your Holy Spirit, hosho...

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

Lord Messiah, our God, who perfected your holy apostles with the perfect gifts of the Holy Spirit, on this memorial of your beloved St. Thomas the Apostle, may you accept the prayers and petitions that we make before you like pleasing incense, shower us with the good gift of your grace, adorn us with your holy and perfect blessings, and strengthen us with your resplendent gifts, May we sing praise unceasingly to You, Your Father, and Your Holy Spirit, hosho...

C:

Amen
