Supplication

P:

O great God who has no beginning or end, and who keeps Your promises true to those who love You, through Your Son Jesus Christ, You accomplished the redemption for sin. You are the Sustainer of life for all, the Refuge to all who put their trust in You and the Helper to those who seek You. O Lord, who sent the Holy Spirit to the disciples as tongues of fire at the third hour to empower them, fill us always with this divine gift, and enrich us through it. Deliver us from all our private and overt sins, and cleanse all our thoughts that are displeasing to You. Make us worthy to thank You for all Your graciousness towards us and to live in Your presence with a pure heart and a virtuous mind.

May we be made worthy...

Praise be to You, O invisible and self-existent One who is obscure and unfathomable to all human thoughts and emotions, and the perfection of power, love and goodness. You are the One who created everything that is visible and invisible, and maintains order. You have no beginning or end, You are eternal and the One for whom no boundary can be set. You are the One true God: the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit; One in essence, One in authority and with One will. There is no name other than that of ‘Three Persons’ and no distinction between them. If you say ‘Father’, then the Son and the Spirit are included. If you say ‘Son’, then the Father and the Spirit are also known through Him. If you say ‘Spirit’, then the Father and the Son make Him complete. The Father is the Creator. The Son is begotten of Him. The Holy Spirit proceeds from the Father, is drawn from the Son and is equal with the Father in essence. We ascribe praise and thanksgiving to the perfect Triune God; God the Father who is the first in the Trinity, the Son and the Holy Spirit, at this time of the commemoration of Pentecost and all the days of our lives, forever more.

Amen

Sedra

P:

O One true God who is invisible, beyond scrutiny, immeasurable and eternal, You created mankind in Your likeness. When they exercised their own free will and went astray to worship the devil, You chose to restore them according to Your holy will and sent Your Son, the Word. Without change to His divine nature, He became man. He enabled us to truly grasp the aspects of Your divinity that were beyond our understanding, and interpreted it clearly to us. He taught us about You, about Himself who came from You and revealed the inexplicable divine mystery, that the Holy Spirit who is the third Light in the Godhead, also proceeded from You. The Holy Spirit is the true Spirit and the Comforter, and is noble, virtuous, wise and valiant. The Holy Spirit creates perfection, is all-sufficient, immeasurable, limitless and loves humanity. The Holy Spirit inspired the prophets, and instilled wisdom in the apostles. The Holy Spirit crowns the clerics, grants understanding to teachers of the Word, establishes Churches, perfects divine worship and extols the priesthood. The Holy Spirit holds many varied gifts for His ministry, is the source of all virtue and the treasury of goodness. He speaks clearly of things that are obscure, predicts the future and works miracles. The Holy Spirit convicts us of sin, brings about repentance and confirms believers. In essence, He is equal to You and to Your only Son. On the heavenly throne of Your kingdom, He is appropriately seated with You and has spoken through the law, the prophets and the apostles. He stays alongside everything and makes everything perfect. O Lord our God, on this great Feast of Pentecost, Your servants worship You, and pray that You pour on us the gift of the Holy Spirit. Fill us with Your divine wisdom as You did in the holy apostles, so that we may understand the mystery of Your divine teachings. Cleanse us from all the blemishes of sin, and enable us to be adorned like sanctified temples to become the abode for Your indwelling. Graciously bestow the Holy Spirit in each one of us. Protect us through His divine ministry, so that we will not succumb to temptations or be harmed by it. Fill us with the Spirit’s fruit of love, joy and peace, through the grace of the Holy Spirit. Enable us to serve You with pure hearts and honour Your commandments. Bestow on us true love and divine power, by unfurling the rays of the Holy Spirit, and save us from the darkness of sin. As we Your servants assemble here to worship You, strengthen and help us to live for You and to complete our lives in You. O God the Father, who is Three in One, we ascribe praise and thanksgiving to You, to Your only Son and to the Holy Spirit who fulfils everything, is blessed, gives life, is equal with You in essence and rules over us, now and forever more.

Supplication

P:

O Lord our Messiah who is blessed by the heavenly hosts, sanctified with the Father and the Holy Spirit, and is the radiant ray of genuine light, You chose to come down to us according to Your holy will and to blend Your likeness with us. You were born in the flesh through the Virgin Mary, to accomplish our salvation through the fulfilment of Your nativity, baptism, passion, death and resurrection. After accomplishing Your salvific deeds and before Your Feast of the Day of Pentecost 200 ascension into heaven, You told Your disciples that unless You leave, another Counsellor will not come to them. You then ascended into heaven after You instructed them to remain in Jerusalem until the Holy Spirit was sent to them from the Father. According to Your promise, on the Day of Pentecost, the Holy Spirit came suddenly and rested on each of them, granted them divine gifts, adorned them with divine wisdom, enlightened their minds with the counsel of the Holy Spirit, and blessed them with the gift of speaking in many tongues. O Lord, today as we celebrate this day of remembrance at the Feast of Pentecost, we appeal to Your inexpressible love for humanity, and pray that You shower us with the grace of the divine and life-giving Holy Spirit. Make us worthy to be cleansed from all the defilement of sin Produce in us the virtuous fruits of faith, hope and love. Grant reconciliation, peace and unity to Your holy Church. Remember the sick and the distressed, the orphans and widows. We ascribe praise and thanksgiving to You, to Your Father and to the Holy Spirit, now and forever more. Amen

Supplication

P:

O Lord God who is the ‘Parakletos’, we worship You. Dwell in us always, like You did in Your holy disciples and enable Your divine endeavours to be always evident in our thoughts, words and behaviour. Do not withhold Your grace from us because of our sins and injustice, and do not deny us Your help. Fill our hearts with Your wisdom, purity and holiness. With Your blessings and grace, enable us to become useful instruments in Your service, and make us temples that are worthy to be the abodes of Your glory. Enable us all to cling onto Your divine wisdom and to continuously offer You pure worship throughout our lives. At Your second coming, make us worthy to receive You with all Your saints. O comforting Holy Spirit who sanctifies our souls, we offer praise and thanksgiving to You, to the Father from whom You proceeded and to the Son with whom You are worshipped. We will always bow down in worship at all times, now and forever more.
