Opening Prayer

P:

O thou, the Father of blessings and mercies, the one who attended to the thanksgiving and praise of the Ninevites, and by his grace looked upon them, wiped away their iniquities, satisfied them with the water of the drink that quenches their thirst, make haste to respond to our appeals, for we supplicate you with the voice of sorrow for our errors, and may you remove your wrath from us.

To the Lord Most High who delights in the the sinners who knock at his door, who rejoices in penitents who await his comfort, who recieved the stray ones who return unto him, who loves the scattered who gather at his harbour, who strengthens the feeble who take refuge in his strength, who raises up the fallen who seek his majesty, to him belongs praise and honor and worship at this time, on all holy days, seasons, and times, and all the days of our lives. Sedra

P:

Lord God, Creator of all creatures, King of kings, Boundless in divine gifts, abundant in good gifts, who hearkens to the petitions of those who knock at the door of repentence with broken and humble hearts, take heed of your servant who worship prostrate before you. Recieve our petition, and answer unto us with grace and kindness from the heights of your holiness. Let not our voices be met with silence. Look upon the brokeness of our hearts before you. Reject us not from your blessings. Recieve with care the petitions aand prayers that we submit to you. May you show yourself to us as the one who recieves us with joy and grants us peace, the one who comforts us. O Lord, who is good and loves goodness, grant us those things which sustain the stability and growth of our life. Stretch out your right hand, which is good and full of blessings, and gladden your creatures. Let fruits about from rain and dew. Delight the face of the earth by your grace. By your grace, grant freely peace and tranquility to the whole world. Remove harsh punishments and the rod of wrath from your creatures. Put an end to wars and conflicts. By your good gifts, make the rich prosperous, and our shower the poor with blessings and glaadden their hearts. Comfort those in sickness and weakness, and deliver those in affliction and various trials. Comfort those who mourn. Encourage the downhearted. Return the lost to the knowledge of the truth. Gather the scattered. Bring back all people to the true way of your commandments. Save us all from the trials of this world, and the agony of the world to come. May we inherit the kingdom of heaven with all the saints, O Father Son and Holy Spirit, and with them and bear praises of vicory to you with voices of gratitude, hosho...

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

Our Lord, the Merciful One, the Most High, Who is concerned with his creation, who have mercy on his worshippers and waits their return, who comforts the brokenhearted, who recieved the repentence of the Ninevites, when they returned because of his prophet Jonah, and removed the just punishment that was set over them, who recieved the repentnce of evildoers, recieve now our incense and prayer according to your mercies, forgive us our debts and sins. Protect us, O Father Son and Holy Spirit, within our mother the Church, that we may give thanks to you and glorify you, hosho...

C:

Amen
