that by external rituals

around the table on that night

and you shall certainly be saved

and go to the land of Canaan

Suthara Prayer

P:

Messiah, our God, who is true, who alone is perfect love, who by your love descended from heaven and walked the earth [ഭൂമിയിൽ വ്യാപരിച്ചു], who in your person [ക്നൂമ] revealed humility and the true path to perfection, who created all things by your eternal power, who sustains them by your word, the Giver of holiness and life, in that house where you held the feast with your disciples, in order to teach them humility by example, you rose from supper, tied a towel aroudn your waist, took water in a basin, and washed their feet. Teach us, O Lord, to likewise serve one another with the unity of love and genuine humility. Wash us of every stain of body and soul. Remove from us every bitterness, anger, jealousy, envy, immoderation [ദുൎവ്വാശി], conflict, corrupt talk and all evil deeds. O Lord, may we delight in the joy of this your festival, he satisfied by the fruits of your Gospel, and bear praise and thanksgiving to you, your father, and the Holy Spirit, hosho...
