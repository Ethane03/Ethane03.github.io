Bathe D'Hasha

(Brik mar hashak d'havah l'appein
brik mukakak methul aasaan)

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

Prepare my harp - to sing verses
of passion with - the Spirit's hand
I come to the field - of your suffering
to reap the sheaves - of death, and keep
this gift of grace inside of me

✠ O Lord, our God...

For those who love the Son of God
Are his suff'rings not life's harbor?
When the ne-ws of - your death is proclaimed
The oppression of the devil
is made freedom - by your suff'ring

✠ O Lord, our God...

If he came in Divinity
Who could stand before His presence?
Of His holy will - He chose to take flesh
He bore suff'ring to give us life
And by love he accepted death

✠ O Lord, our God...

Because you tasted death, Jesus
in order to give sinners life
Grant ev'ry blessing - of salva-tion
That you alone won on the cross
that we should not fall to Evil

✠ O Lord, our God...

Ba'usa of Mar Jacob

(M'shiha dezal hashau mautha vaskippusa
shma ba'usaan vabed rahme 'al napshasaan)

Christ who came to suffer death on the cursed cross
Hear our prayer and declare your grace over us

✠ O Lord, our God...

You are the high priest, and the sacrifice offered
You are the Firstborn and the unblemished firstfruits
Both the gift, and the one to whom gifts are given
The lamb of sacrifice and priest who offers it

✠ O Lord, our God...

You came to Jerusalm on a donkey's back
and wept when you saw the destruction of the Jews
Your servants wipe away your tears by repentance
Grant us aid, so that your wrath might be extinguished

✠ O Lord, our God...

Lord Jesus, you redeemed sinners by sacrifice
On your cross, you undid all our condemnation
O Lord God whose heart was pierced atop Golgotha
May your servants who drank your blood be given life

✠ O Lord, our God...

Bless'd is the Father who sent his son to free us
Bless'd is the Son who rebuked Israel's daughter
Bless'd is the Spirit who built the church for worship
O Holy Trinity, listen to us sinners

✠ O Lord, our God...

Answer O God, Answer O God and have mercy
May you turn the minds of men toward repentance

✠ O Lord, our God...

Prayer of St. Severus

P:

O Lord who sits in the secret place of the Most High, shelter us beneath the shadow of the wings of Thy mercy, and have compassion upon us. Thou, who hearest all things, in Thy loving kindness, hearken to the supplications of Thy servants. Grant us, O Messiah; our Savior; a peaceful evening and a sinless night, for Thou art a glorious king, and unto Thee, are our eyes lifted up. Forgive our debts and our sins; have mercy upon us, both in this world and in that to come. May Thy loving kindness shelter us O Lord, and Thy grace be upon our faces. May Thy cross protect us from the evil one and his hosts. Let Thy right hand overshadow us all the days of our lives, and Thy peace reign among us, do Thou give hope and salvation to the souls that pray to Thee. O God, forgive us our debts, and have mercy upon us. Amen.
