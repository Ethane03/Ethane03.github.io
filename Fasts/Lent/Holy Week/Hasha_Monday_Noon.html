Bathe D'Hasha

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

The news of your death, Son of God
Has been proclaimed from Moses on
The wicked ones who - crucified you Lord
and shed you blood are now branded
with the curse of Cain on their heads

✠ O Lord, our God...

If he had blazed in his glory
Who on earth could have tested Him?
God has appeared in - the flesh of mortals
The foolish nation of the Jews
does dare to look upon their Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Maker of heaven and earth
every mouth shall give praise to You
Adam is renewed - into your image
Praise be to You, and your father
and the Holy Spirit Amen

✠ O Lord, our God...

Ba'usa of Mar Ephrem

Lord who suffering agonies
have mercy upon us all
that we might partake in them
And inherit Your kingdom

✠ O Lord, our God...

The Lord made an example
of a fig tree to the Jews
When he came to it, hungry
He cursed it for lack of fruit
By this he made a figure
of the church of Israel

✠ O Lord, our God...

The Faithful and Holy Church
Praises you,Lord, at all times
She worships the Trinity
blessing you and your Father,
who sent You to rescue us
And she praises the Spirit

✠ O Lord, our God...

O hearer of our prayers
O granter of petitions
May you hear our prayers and
May you grant our petitions
