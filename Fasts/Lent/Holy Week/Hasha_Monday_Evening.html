Bathe D'Hasha

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

Your grace compelled you to renew
the race of Adam in your form
To restore them to - Eden's paradise
You put on their flesh and suffered
For their sake because of Your grace

✠ O Lord, our God...

You are the One who descended
From heaven to the transgressors
And you showered us - with loving-mercy
You humbled yourself unto death
And by suff'ring You made us free

✠ O Lord, our God...

In hunger, you came to the tree
And saw no fruit on it's branches
You cursed the fig tree - and made it wither
and made a lesson for the Jews.
O Lord may you not curse us too!

✠ O Lord, our God...

We too are trees in your garden
Bearing no fruit, but only leaves
It is right to take - us from your garden
O Gardener, plow and water [lit. put fertilizer]
and help us to bear fruit for you.

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Good One, Lord Omnipotent
You bore affliction and the cross
To give life to us. - Glory forever
be to You and to your Father
And the Holy Spirit, amen
