Bathe D'Hasha

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

Your grace compelled you to renew
the race of Adam in your form
To restore them to - Eden's paradise
You put on their flesh and suffered
For their sake because of Your grace

✠ O Lord, our God...

You are the One who descended
From heaven to the transgressors
And you showered us - with loving-mercy
You humbled yourself unto death
And by suff'ring You made us free

✠ O Lord, our God...

In hunger, you came to the tree
And saw no fruit on it's branches
You cursed the fig tree - and made it wither
and made a lesson for the Jews.
O Lord may you not curse us too!

✠ O Lord, our God...

We too are trees in your garden
Bearing no fruit, but only leaves
It is right to take - us from your garden
O Gardener, plow and water [lit. put fertilizer]
and help us to bear fruit for you.

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Good One, Lord Omnipotent
You bore affliction and the cross
To give life to us. - Glory forever
be to You and to your Father
And the Holy Spirit, amen

✠ O Lord, our God...

Ba'usa of Mar Jacob

Christ who came to suffer death on the cursed cross
Hear our prayer and declare your grace over us

✠ O Lord, our God...

Good One, who was rejected by his own people
were you despised because you descended to them?
You, the righteous One, recieved insult from sinners
And although you could destroy them, you forgave them
You did not hate them though they heaped curses on you
You adorned yourself with love and humulity
When you hungered and desired pick good fruit
They gave you bitterness and hung you on a tree

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Lord, did you not untie the tounges of the mute?
With those same tounges they now spit in your holy face
You cast out demons but were called "Chief of demons"
You cured their sick yet they call out in mockery
What a wonder of love is reflected in You
When you remember us, you heart burns within you
Grant us your spirit, to search and to know your love
For by whom, O my Lord, can it be put to words?

✠ O Lord, our God...

Answer O God, Answer O God and have mercy
May you turn the minds of men toward repentance

May the heavens - and the earth all
Sing praise to You - forever, Lord!
Remember us - at your coming
and in your Love - help us O Lord!

The story of - faithful Abel
is pleasant to - the faithful now
And they wonder - at wicked Cain
who was exiled - east of Eden

Though Abel died - the Holy church
ever recalls - his great story
For Cain did spill - his guiltless blood
upon the earth - in jealousy

Abel has gained - a wond'rous name
like the name of - the lamb of God
Even today - the Holy Church
counts him as great - among martyrs

How wonderous - The Son of God
the Lamb that dawned - upon the earth!
The Father sent - a substitute
to take away - all the world's sin

The Children of - Cain were wrathful
they shed His blood - upon the cross
But the children - of Abel did
recieve Him and find salvation

The Sacrifice - of the Son was
an incense that - pleased the Father
The charges of - sinners were gone
And with God they - were reconciled

O Son of God - we make off'rings
recieve them as - you did Abel's
Grant to us all - inheritance
in salvation - by your off'ring

Praise be to you - and the Father
and the Holy - Spirit, Amen.
Grant us ever - inheritance
of your kingdom - to sing praises!
