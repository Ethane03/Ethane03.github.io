Suthara Prayer

P:

Lord Messiah, who created all things by His word, the fount of light and life, the minister who became a servant in order to deliver the human race, the teacher who made wise the foolish disciples, you wrapped a towel around your waist, poured water into a basin, washed the feet of your disciples, and taughht them the true example of humility and love. O Lord, grant us, your servants, grace to follow that example, and to be humble and lovers of eachother. O Lord, remember not our sins, and grant purity to the stains of our bodies and souls. Remove from us our sorrows and lies, jealousy and conflict, and all evil thoughts and evil deeds, Renew and sanctify our souls by the Holy Spirit. May we suffer and forgive one another, honor one another, divide various kinds of efforts of praise, and be united like the members of our bodies. May we all sing praise and exaltation to You, Your Father, and the Holy Spirit, hosho...

C:

Amen.
