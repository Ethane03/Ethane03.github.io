Opening Prayer

P:

O Christ, our Lord, who by your boundless love was crucified for us, and who has made your suffering a strong fortress and a victorious armour for all who take refuge in it, may you accept by your grace this prayer that we offer in your presence. By our meditation on your life-giving Passion, may you remove from us all evils, and all thoughts that are unpleasing to you. May you make us worthy to believe in your Passion, to celebrate and worship it, and to stand at Your right hand, hosho...

Bathe D'Hasha

(Not sung in Bathe D'Hasha Melody)

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

When I survey the wondrous cross
on which the Prince of glory died,
my richest gain I count but loss,
and pour contempt on all my pride.

✠ O Lord, our God...

Forbid it, Lord, that I should boast
save in the death of Christ, my God!
All the vain things that charm me most,
I sacrifice them through his blood.

✠ O Lord, our God...

See, from his head, his hands, his feet,
sorrow and love flow mingled down.
Did e'er such love and sorrow meet,
or thorns compose so rich a crown?

✠ O Lord, our God...

Were the whole realm of nature mine,
that were a present far too small.
Love so amazing, so divine,
demands my soul, my life, my all.

✠ O Lord, our God...

Ba'usa of Mar Ephrem

Lord who suffering agonies
have mercy upon us all
that we might partake in them
And inherit Your kingdom

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Merciful One how great
was your love for your people?
To bring them to Your glory,
You offered your very self.
You put on a lowly form
And descended unto death.
You bore the weight of the cross,
giving them eternal life.

✠ O Lord, our God...

Alas! The judgement is writ
to be hung on Golgotha
They force the cross upon Him
and then lay Him upon it
They fasten Him to that tree
with nails in His hands and feet
And on either side of Him
are crucified criminals

✠ O Lord, our God...

Praise be to you Messiah
Whose suff'ring saved us from error
Praise be to the Father who
sent You to die for our sake
Praise be to the Paraclete
Who raises us up to life

✠ O Lord, our God...

O hearer of our prayers
O granter of petitions
May you hear our prayers and
May you grant our petitions

Madrasha

In the tune of Shethk Sakkaya

He saw the desolate world from Heaven
And he left the glory of the Father
Putting on a servant's clothes
to be come a man He chose
He descended to the cross and he tasted death

Mortals had fallen into Divine wrath
Sinners were vessels for damnation
He became an offering
to save them by blood-shedding
He put out the Father's wrath and made peace with Him

Come before me, O Jesus, with Your cross
That I might look upon the true form of love
All I have is You alone
Without You, who shall I love?
I shall love you O Lord, all the days of my life

By the cross you bought salvation for me
By your blood you won a fortune for me
Now, O Death, where is your sting?
Hell, where is your victory?
At the cross the Lord shed a river of blessing

From now on I shall sing songs of gladness
To the Lord on Earth, and then in Heaven
I shall enter the kingdom
in glory and blessed joy
Then shall you place a crown of gold upon my head
