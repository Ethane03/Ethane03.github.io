Good Friday Morning Prayers : Part II

Prefatory Prayers

Priest:

✠ Glory be to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Congr:

As it was in the beginning is now and ever shall be. Amen.

P:

Holy, Holy, Holy Lord God of power and might, heaven and earth are full of Your glory, Hosanna in the highest.

C:

Blessed is he who has come and is to come again in the name of the Lord. Hosanna in the highest.

Kauma

O Lord our Messiah, who was brought to trial by Thy servant, on the day when Thou judges the world in righteousness, be compassionate and have mercy on us.

Lak'mar theshbuhta

Praise be to Thee O Lord, glory be to the Father
and to the Holy Spirit, honor and worship
Bestow thy blessing and grace
upon us thy sinful servants
O Messiah, open the doors of heavenly Jerusalem
so that our prayers may reach thy throne of grace
My Lord, praise be to thee. My Lord, praise be to thee
Praise be to thee, our refuge eternal - barek

The Lord's Prayer

P:

Our Father in heaven…

C:

Hallowed be Your name. May Your Kingdom come. May Your will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread. Forgive our sins as we forgive those who sin against us. Do not bring us to the time of trial, but deliver us from the evil one. For the Kingdom, the power, and the glory are Yours now and forever. Amen

Ba'usa of Mar Jacob

✠ O Lord, our God...

Go Sinner, to Jesus in dark Gethsemane.
See your Lord exhausted in battle with Satan,
who fires flaming arrows, as the Father places
sin's burden on His Son, become prey for the sword.
He melts in the fires of wrath; the Holy Body sweats blood
In prayer He accepts the will of His Father

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Sinner, draw near to Lord Jesus crucified
Love awakens in the depths of the Heart of God
And overflowing, it pours out over your head
The Holy Face shines bright even in the darkest night
There is not day that the Love of God shall depart
from over You, nor shall the light of God grow dim
O Lord, grant me the light of your Holy Spirit
That by faith I might see clearly the Light of God

The wicked Jews crucified - the world's Saviour

rocks were broken - and the hills trembled

Hymn

(This is a Bathe D'Hasha in the Malayalam)

When I survey the wondrous cross
on which the Prince of glory died,
my richest gain I count but loss,
and pour contempt on all my pride.

✠ O Lord, our God...

Forbid it, Lord, that I should boast
save in the death of Christ, my God!
All the vain things that charm me most,
I sacrifice them through his blood.

✠ O Lord, our God...

See, from his head, his hands, his feet,
sorrow and love flow mingled down.
Did e'er such love and sorrow meet,
or thorns compose so rich a crown?

✠ O Lord, our God...

Were the whole realm of nature mine,
that were a present far too small.
Love so amazing, so divine,
demands my soul, my life, my all.

✠ O Lord, our God...

was your love for your people?

You offered your very self.

giving them eternal life.
