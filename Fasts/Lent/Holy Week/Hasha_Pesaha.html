Sedra I

P:

We worship You, our God and Saviour, Lord Messiah, who was fortold by the prophets, and shown to them by signs. You, the lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world, consumed the lamb of the law with your disciples. Afterward, you took bread in your holy hands, blessed it, broke it, gave it to your disciples and spoke, "Take, eat, this is my body broken for the sins of the world". You took the cup mixed of wine and water, gave it to them, and said "This is my blood of the New Covenant", and spoke "Do this in remembrance of me".

From this time on all that is old is ended, and every thing is made new. Behold, how wonderous is the sacrifice that offers Himself, and proclaims that He will be judged like the lamb! Great is this mystery to the learned, and marvelous is this story [ചരിത്രം] to the wise.

This gift the Church accepts; This mystery she recieves in faith by Sacrament. O Lord, create the desire in Your Church to know Your promises through pure doctrines. Cleanse her from all defilement and stains and clothe her with humility, unity and love. We offer You, the Father and the Holy Spirit, praise and thanksgiving, hosho...

Amen
