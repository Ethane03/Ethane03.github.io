Prefatory Prayers

✠ Glory be to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Congr:

As it was in the beginning is now and ever shall be. Amen.

P:

Holy, Holy, Holy Lord God of power and might, heaven and earth are full of Your glory, Hosanna in the highest.

C:

Blessed is he who has come and is to come again in the name of the Lord. Hosanna in the highest.

At the cross - I hear Sorrow's voice
My saviour cries out on it
There I hear - a great cry of grief
In it are all things fulfilled
Sacrifice gave me freedom
Satan's head was crushed by it
Jesus' hand holds victory
The Lord left - the shining heavens
And won here a great vict'ry

Men olam...

Here I am - by your side, Jesus
I behold you on the cross
I pour tears - on your holy feet
O my Lord I cannot speak
In this world has no one e'er
loved sinners in such a way
But Christ has loved me even
Unto death - and he protects me
Driving off my enemies

Quqaya

The Son of God bows His head - atop Golgotha
He commits His spirit in - to the Father's hands
The Immortal - goes to bring comfort
In Hades to - Adam's descendents
Who fell asleep in the hope - of resurrection
Christ shows the way out of death - into paradise
Hallelujah w'Hallelujah

Shubaho...

Men olam...

God ascended the ladder - and He tasted death
He descended to Hades - and gave sinners life
He destroyed its - gates and all its walls
He broke apart - Sheol's iron lock
Praise be forever to Him - who raised Adam up
From destruction into life - as He had promised
Hallelujah w'Hallelujah

Moryo Rahem...

Bathe D'Hasha

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

Israel was a grapevine brought
out of Egpyt by pow'r of old
He sweetened waters of Marah for her
Yet when He wished to drink she gave
Bitter vinegar on a sponge

✠ O Lord, our God...

At the ninth hour the Lord of Love
lays hold of the mighty devil
Abolishing fears; casting out sorrows
He makes atonement unto God
and cries aloud "it is finished"

✠ O Lord, our God...

The rocks shatter and tear apart
The graves are opened up to life
In the great temple, the curtain is torn
in two peices whie the sun hides
its face and darkens its daylight

✠ O Lord, our God...

At the cross a soldier declares
"Truly He is the Son of God"
The executioner spears Christ in the side
His heart opens, pouring out blood
and water to purify the world

✠ O Lord, our God...

Ba'usa of Mar Aprem

Lord, who suffered agonies,
On Your servants have mercy
that we might partake in them
And inherit Your kingdom

✠ O Lord, our God...

To fulfill the written word
In a body came the Lord
Bearing burdens willingly
He was numbered among thieves
Nails tore through His hands and feet
blood shed from His whole body

✠ O Lord, our God...

The burning light of the sun
oppresses the Holy One
But the greater pain is when
The Father's face is hidden
"My God, Eloi", He weeps
In worship, His Father seeks

✠ O Lord, our God...

Like rain fall Satan's arrows
Enemies surround the cross
What was stolen by Satan
is taken back by the Son
The rich plunder of his schemes
the Almighty now redeems

✠ O Lord, our God...

The Father bestows on Him
Ev'ry blessing in heaven
The whole world is made His keep
to its furthest boundary
Singly has He defeated
The Devil, Flesh and the World
By his blood he reconciled
God the Father with mankind

✠ O Lord, our God...

He cried out "It is finished"
And soon after bows His head
To the Father His Spirit
The faithful Son now commits
Who is able to explain
the victory that you have gained?
For the church an order make*
In your vict'ry to partake*

✠ O Lord, our God...

Lord, who listens to our pray'rs
Lord, who grants our petitions
May you hear all our prayers
and grant us our petitions

✠ O Lord, our God...

Madrasha

In the tune of Shethk Sakkaya

O Sinner, the Father has sent to you
A Physician to cure your diseases
This man is the one bears
all the world's iniquity
He became Oblation to free us by His blood

He bids you, "come near to me on the cross"
to see his body and to kiss His feet
Remember, one of the thieves
And the Centurion saw
The glory of God shine from within Jesus Christ

Look! Sinners are coming near to the cross
Defeated enemies scatter and hide
We see the cross' victory
And we bow down to beseech
that you pour out rivers of blood for purity

He says that salvation is completed
The Father beolds His Son's offering
He takes away the curses
And showers Him in blessings
The darkness runs away; Let us run to the cross

O Sinner, repent and leave behind sin
There is no other refuge for sinners
Glory be to the Father
the Son and Holy Spirit
Unto the ages of ages and evermore
