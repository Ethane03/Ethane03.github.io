Bathe D'Hasha

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

At evening on this day Joseph
went to visit Pontius Pilate
asking to remove from the Gallows
The body of the Messiah
his request was quickly granted

✠ O Lord, our God...

With the rich man Nicodemus
and the devoted Women joined
They took the body of Jesus with care
Down from the cross, seeing His wounds
They washed all of His suffering

✠ O Lord, our God...

With costly strips of linen cloth
They wrapped the body of the Lord
With honour they placed Him in Joseph's grave
Which was newly cut from the rock
and covered it with a great stone

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Loving Lord, Jesus the Christ
how amazing is the sweet sight
of the grieving ones casting off their doubts
With love desiring to bury
your lovely and holy body

✠ O Lord, our God...

With You shall I also enter
the grave, O Lord, as a dead man.
As you were raised up on the third day
So may I be raised up with you
to dwell on high in the Kingdom

✠ O Lord, our God...

Ba'usa of Mar Jacob

Christ who came to suffer death on the cursed cross
Hear our prayer and declare your grace over us

✠ O Lord, our God...

O Holy Church come quickly and behold the Cross
Whoever sees the holy body shall cry out
in tears, "Who, oh who is this man crucified?"
Why, oh why did this happen to you, O my Lord
You allow your back to be plowed with long furrows
You offer your cheek to those who had torn your hair
You bestow your Holy face to those who beat you
The spearman makes your holy bosom his target
Your heart opens and blood and water flow from it

✠ O Lord, our God...

What a wond'rous thing I behold in you, O God
I am unable to describe your holy wounds
Like a great mountain did you bear all of my sin
And it dissolved into a great ocean of love

✠ O Lord, our God...

In order to wash away all of my blemishes
You have poured out your blood as a holy fountain
I come to be crucified with You, Jesus Christ
And there I behold, O Lord, the depths of your love

✠ O Lord, our God...

Ocean of love, your love is pouring into me
and my heart thirst to drink of You, Lord Messiah
The one who does not love you recieves great curses
But the one who loves You recieves many blessings

✠ O Lord, our God...

Answer O God, Answer O God and have mercy
May you turn the minds of men toward repentance

✠ O Lord, our God...

Bathe D'Hasha

Blessed be the shame and suff'ring
that You bore for our sake, O Lord

✠ O Lord, our God...

When Adam first slept in Eden
God from his side formed a woman
Adam awoke and - saw her before him
"At last, here is flesh of my flesh
bone of my bone", proclaimed Adam

✠ O Lord, our God...

The second Adam slept upon
the cross on top of Golgotha
The Church was redeemed by His death that day
By grace, O Lord, give aid to Her
and car for Her in purity

✠ O Lord, our God...

The Father saw the Son's suff'ring
and with compassion said to Him
Ask and I shall give what you desire
I will make the nations your keep
The whole world will be your kingdom

✠ O Lord, our God...

Your servants partake in Your death
and are buried beside you, Lord
May we follow you in resurrection
grant us new life and in the end
let us partake in Your glory

✠ O Lord, our God...

Praise be forever to You, Lord
For You have won us by Your death
May we be seated beside You, Jesus
may we be made worthy to praise
the Father and Holy Spirit

Ba'usa of Mar Ephrem

Lord who suffering agonies
have mercy upon us all
that we might partake in them
And inherit Your kingdom

✠ O Lord, our God...

Come, O Sinner and draw near
to the cross of the Savior
Come, let us weep many tears
and speak to Love incarnate
Come to the court of judgement
let us follow after Him
See the Lord of Life as He
recieves the death penalty
In the Jews' assembly
He recieves blows merc'lessly
His body is subject to
pain, suff'ring, and many wounds
In rage they take whips and clubs
and lay beatings upon Him
Like the despised goat does he
suffer all without hatred

✠ O Lord, our God...

He humbled himself before
enemies, even to death
Let us take up our crosses
and approach Him to learn how
to bear suffring pain and shame
and thus follow after Him
O great Teacher and Savior
Praise be to you by your cross
Praise be to you, O Father
who sent your Son for sinners
And the Holy Church ever
praises the Holy Spirit
