Sedra

P:

Lord God, the Living Word, who surpasses all knowledge, you became fully man, having compassion on your own who were trapped in the nets of Satan. Though you dwelt on the earth, at the Unity of Divinity and Humanity, the leaders of the Jews did not recognize you, nor heed your word. O Beautiful One, who came to restore our ancient beauty, you were called unlovely among men. You bore our sins in your body, and healed us, that we might die unto sin and live unto righteousness. Without fleeing from bondage or judgement, as fulfillment of prophecy, you gave up your body to rods, your cheek to beatings, and your face to spit. You gave up your hands and feet to be fixed by nails, your side to be pierced, your mouth to taste bitter vinegar, and your clothes to be divided. You were hung on the cross of spite. You were counted among the unjust. Thus the Father's will was fulfilled in You.

Therefore, we pray to You, O Lord, remove from us all unbearable suffering and sorrow! Grant us complete repentance from evil works, and forgiveness of sins. We offer praise and thanksgiving to You, Your Father, and Your Holy Spirit, hosho...

C:

Amen.
