and knelt before your disciples

love one another
that the world might know
[what the true love of God is]
O Lord grant us spiritual gifts
so that we might walk in your Way

Today Jesus, with parables
pronounced teaching before the Jews
But they did not understand him
for their minds were uncircumsised
The lord prophesied
damnation for them
and announced judgement
He showed their duty to them
God stretched out the hand of mercy
"If you repent, you will be saved"

May all your parables, Jesus
lead your servants that they fall not
into the way of destruction
and help us to imitate the Lord
Make us worthy to
stand on judgement day
before your great throne
with bright faces and gladness
Extend the wings of your mercy
Hide us and do not be wrathful
