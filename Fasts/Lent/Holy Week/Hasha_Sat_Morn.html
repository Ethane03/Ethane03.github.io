The Lord's Prayer

Opening Prayer

O Lord our God, who neither slumbers nor sleeps, wake us from our deep slumber of sin, so that we may praise Your watchfulness over us. Immortal and living God, arouse us from the sleep of death and destruction, so that we may adore Your mercy. O Lord, who is the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, because You alone are praised and glorified in heaven and on earth, make us also worthy to praise and worship You, together with all the majestic angelic hosts who in holiness, praise You in heaven.

Amen

Psalms

Bless the Lord, all you servants of the Lord...

Enyana

O God, who willingly embraced death and by Your death, gave life to the dead,

have mercy on us

O God, who by very nature is life itself, but was buried among the dead for our sake

have mercy on us

O God, because You are Immortal, and yet died, came back to life, and granted us life,

have mercy on us

O God, who was placed among the dead like a criminal because of sinful Adam,

have mercy on us

O God, who remained in the tomb for three days for our salvation,

have mercy on us

O God, who by descending to hell, gained victory over death, discharged us and cancelled the verdict,

have mercy on us

O God, who was buried in the tomb like a dead person, but as God, granted life to those who are dead,

have mercy on us

O God, who by Your entry into the tomb, brought honour to those who had fallen asleep in hell,

have mercy on us

Promeon

Let us all...

O Lord of all blessings...

May we be made worthy...
O Lord our Messiah, who by very nature is life and the giver of life, the refuge for the dead, the resurrection for those buried in the tomb, the delight of those in the grave, the radiance that dispels darkness, the solace for the distressed, the obliterator of death, the destroyer of hell, the restorer of Your image and the upholder of Your creation, You are worthy of praise, honour and adoration, at this time of worship and all the days of our lives, forever more.

Amen

Sedra

O Son of the invisible Father, God the Word, who became fully Man though without sin, we adore You. Since You are by very nature, life itself and You give life, we will always praise You because of Your high regard and love for us that made You suffer death on the cross, to save us from the death of sin. We worship Your mercy because, by Your own will, You lay in the grave to free us from the depths of hell, like a Saviour. We praise Your love that made You come down unassumingly among the dead, so that we who were destroyed by death could live by the glow of the divine light. We honour Your compassion that made You come down in person to deliver Adam and his descendants who had fallen into the deep pit. We proclaim Your enormous grace and divine willingness with which You delivered us from the grasp of the oppressor. O Lord, today the Church, Your true bride, celebrates the victory of Your death and worship Your passion and crucifixion. Our Saviour, Lord and Messiah, fulfil the promises to the holy Church. Bless her children. Establish her among her future generations. Fill her with abundant peace. Enable all her petitions to be fulfilled. May Your right hand be upon her. Sustain Your compassion among her children. May Your blessings be a refuge, and Your mercy a high fortress for her. Shower Your abundant blessings on her. May Your Holy Spirit always dwell in her. O only begotten God, she, together with her posterity, in complete communion, praise You for Your suffering, death and burial for her sake, and worship and adore You, the Father who sent You to save us, and the life-giving Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

Amen

Ethra

Our Lord, the epitome of life, who gave life to the dead by Your salvific death, removed the power of sin, and for the saved, transformed it into sleep, remove all sinful deeds and completely fill our entire being, with a faultless and pure life. Spread the fragrance of good deeds on the functioning of our minds. Enable us to praise and thank You, the Father and the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

Amen

Supplications

O God our Father and Lord of all, we thank You for bringing us safely to this moment. In Your mercy, keep us safe from sin and danger today and sustain us with Your righteousness. O Lord, because You are our only refuge, bestow on us Your perfect love and great mercy. Because You are a merciful Lord, teach us Your law and keep us on its path. O holy Lord, allow us to shine forth with Your light. Bestow upon us all the goodness we need today. You are worthy of praise, worship and sovereignty.

Amen

O Most High One, it is good to thank You O Lord, to sing praises to Your name, to sing of Your mercies in the morning and to proclaim Your faithfulness in the evening. O Lord, listen to our voice in the morning; in the morning, we will come prepared to be in Your presence. O Lord, have mercy on Your people. O Lord, absolve and forgive us all, our sins. O holy One, stretch forth Your right hand upon us. In Your holy name, heal us also from the diseases of sin.

Amen
