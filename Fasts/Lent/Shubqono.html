Praise be to thee, O Father, the heavenly peace. Laud be to thee, O Son, the peace of earth. Honor be to thee, O Holy Spirit, fulfiller of the Divine Gifts. The Father is the One who gave His Son unto Death for our salvation. The Son is the One who descended by His love and died for our sake. The Holy Spirit is the One who rejoices in our goodness and by whom our petitions are granted. God is One in Essence and Three in Person. O One Lord, help us to sincerely be at peace with one another. My you guard us all in divine unity with you and in the bonds of love. You are worthy of praise and honor and glory on the joyful day of your glorious resurrection, at this time, on all holy days, seasons, and times, and all the days of our lives. Hosho...

Sedra

P:

O God our Messiah, who is the unwavering peace for all of creation, and the genuine peace that abides across all frontiers and nations, You are Love! You are called ‘Love’ and You rejoice in this name above all other names. You are known for the love You have for humanity, and You honour those who accept Your love. Through Your cross, You removed the barriers that created animosity, and with Your own blood, You reconciled those in heaven with those on earth. You have reconciled these two groups and abolished the hostility between them through the essence of the indivisible union between Your divinity and Your transient humanity. You preached peace and unity to the world, called those near and far, united them in the bond of Your divine love, and offered them the inheritance of Your eternal kingdom. O our Saviour, protect us with this love and may it govern the impulses of our intellect. Bind us together in Your love. Through Your love, eliminate the discord and fractious disputes that separate us. Through Your love, make us worthy to observe Your commandments. Through Your love, eradicate sorrow and distress from our midst. Through Your love, make us children of love and peace. Through the message of peace brought by Your archangel, You revealed Your conception to Your virgin mother. In peace, Your mother visited Elizabeth, the mother of John who preached about You, and the joy of hearing Your words of peace, made him leap and rejoice, as a servant before his master. At Your birth, the angels preached peace to the shepherds. You granted peace to the women who longed for Your glorious resurrection. In peace You entered the upper room where Your apostles had gathered, and confirmed Your resurrection to them. When You ascended to the One who sent You, You granted peace to Your apostles by proclaiming, “My peace I give to You” and directed them to “Love one another as I have loved you”. Through Your peace, unite Your shepherds with their flocks and enable the priests to rejoice in their sacraments. Through Your peace, make Your holy Church and her children perfect, unite her leaders and bring back those who have strayed. Through Your peace, regulate her organisations and integrate her great festivals. Protect Your parish with Your peace, purify our thoughts and keep us on the right path. May Your peace always be with us, and may divine unity reign among us daily. Bind our hearts with Your love and enable our thoughts to be focused on Your heavenly peace. Unite and adorn us with Your peace at all feasts, and accept our Lent and supplications. With Your peace, pacify those who are angry, and adorn the aged. Protect us from merciless superiors with Your peace, and graciously grant us deliverance. Break the chains of our bondage to the evil one with Your peace, and remove his yoke from us. O Lord of peace, enable us to thank You, Your Father who grants peace and the Holy Spirit who perfects true peace, hosho...

C:

Amen

Reconciliation

The priest turns to face the people, saying:

P:

My beloved brothers and sisters, through the Messiah, please forgive me of the wrongs the I have committed against You. I have counselled as a watchful shephard, with Spiritual unity and Fatherly love, and I now bow my head before you.

C:

Lord have mercy upon us, we await forgiveness

P:

Come, my beloved bretheren, I appeal to your true love. Let us reconcile with one another that God may be reconciled with us. I bow down in honor of the Holy Tinity.

C:

Lord have mercy upon us, we await forgiveness

P:

With a broken heart and a pure conscience before all of you, I appeal to your love, crying out with a loud voice. My beloved bretheren, come and let us be reconciled with one another. Each of us must forgive your brothers and sister of the wrongs that you have commited against eachother, we might be forgiven of our sins, and that we might be reconciled to God as he has said, "If you forgive eachother your faults and failings, you also shall be forgiven". May the Lord God forgive my sins and yours.

C:

Lord have mercy upon us, we await forgiveness

One person from the congregation comes forward and says the following:

L:

Our respected father in the Lord and God’s anointed one, if we have in any way grieved or caused you pain, we bow our heads, requesting you to forgive us in God’s name. Do not cease to pray for us.

P:

God’s people who are redeemed by the holy blood of the Messiah, may the Lord forgive our sins and help us to abound in His grace.

The Kiss of Peace

C:

(Repeating) May the love and peace of our Lord Jesus Christ, abide with us forever

Benediction

P:

Blessed be the beginning and end of this Holy Lent. May God aid and support all of you to obey His Will and to complete the fast. Make he clear out from before you the nets of Satan, opposing faithful Christians, and of enemies secret and public. As our Lord Jesus the Messiah at the end of His fast rebuked Satan 'Get behind me', so may He guard you and save you from all of the trials of Satan.

C:

Amen
