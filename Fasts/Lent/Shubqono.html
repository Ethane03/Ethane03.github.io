Sedra

P:

O Messiah, our God, who is the invincible peace of all creation, and the true tranquility reaching to the ends of the earth, You are Love! You are called love, and you rejoice in this name above all other names. Becuse of love, you are known as the friend of mankind, and You honour those who accept this love. Through Your cross, You removed the barriers of enmity, and with Your own blood, You reconciled those in heaven and those on earth. You have reconciled these two and abolished the hostility between them through indivisible union between Your essential divinity and Your temporal humanity. By love you called those near and far, bound them in the unity of Your divine love and in the inheritance of Your eternal kingdom, and You preached peace and unity to the world. Through the word of peace brought by Your archangel, You revealed Your conception to Your virgin mother. In peace, Your mother visited Elizabeth, the mother of John who preached about You, and the joy of hearing Your words of peace, made him leap and rejoice, as a servant before his master. At Your birth, the angels preached peace to the shepherds. You granted peace to the women who longed for Your glorious resurrection. In peace You entered the upper room where Your apostles had gathered, and confirmed Your resurrection to them. When You ascended to the One who sent You, You granted peace to Your apostles by proclaiming, “My peace I give to You” and commanded them, “Love one another as I have loved you”. O our Saviour, protect us with this love, and by it may you steady our minds. Bind us to each other in this love. Through Your love, eliminate the discord and disputes that separate us. Through Your love, make us worthy to observe Your commandments. Through Your love, eradicate sorrow and distress from our midst. Through Your love, make us children of love and peace. Through Your peace, unite Your shepherds with their flocks and gladden the priests in their sacraments. Through Your peace, make perfect Your holy Church and her children, and goven them by your peace. By your tranquility, adorn your church. By your peace, delight her children. By your peace, gather those who are far and protect those who are near. Make her servants resplendent, and unite her leaders. Through Your peace, regulate her ranks and order her great feasts. By your peace, pacify the wrathful, and adorn the elderly. By your peace, fill the distressed with joy. Protect Your parish with Your peace, purify our thoughts. Protect us from merciless superiors with Your peace. Unite and adorn us with Your peace at all feasts, and accept our Lent and supplications. By your peace, grant us grace and forgiveness. Break the chains of our bondage to the evil one with Your peace, and remove his yoke from us. May Your peace always be with us, and may divine unity reign among us daily. Bind our hearts with Your love and enable our thoughts to be focused on Your heavenly peace. O Lord of peace, enable us to thank You, Your Father who grants peace and the Holy Spirit who perfects true peace, hosho...

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

Our Lord, make our hearts the abode of peace and our thoughts the harbour of tranquillity. Sow within us true love towards You. Establish love, unity and harmony among us. Make it possible for us to sincerely bring peace to each other and to recive it in sincerity. May we please Your holy will, as choice incense and sweet frangrances. O Lord, whose peace brings joy to all things, and whose unity delights all things, together with Your Father and the Holy Spirit, to you belongs majesty and honour, hosho...

Amen.

Reconciliation

The priest turns to face the people, saying:

P:

My beloved brothers and sisters, on account of the Messiah, please forgive me of the wrongs the I have committed against You. I have counselled as a watchful shephard, with Spiritual unity and Fatherly love, and I now bow my head before you.

C:

Barekmor, we await forgiveness

P:

Come, my beloved bretheren, I appeal to your true love. Let us reconcile with one another that God may be reconciled with us. I bow down in honor of the Holy Tinity.

C:

Barekmor, we await forgiveness

P:

With a broken heart and a pure conscience before all of you, I appeal to your love, crying out with a loud voice: My beloved bretheren, come and let us be reconciled with one another. Each of us must forgive our brothers and sister of the wrongs that we have commited against eachother, so that we might be forgiven of our sins, and that we might be reconciled to God as he has said, "If you forgive eachother your faults and failings, you also shall be forgiven". May the Lord God forgive my sins and yours.

C:

Barekmor, we await forgiveness

One person from the congregation comes forward and says the following:

L:

Our reverend father in the Lord and God’s anointed one, if we have in any way grieved or caused you pain, we bow our heads, requesting you to forgive us in God’s name. Do not cease to pray for us.

P:

People of God, who are redeemed by the holy blood of the Messiah, may the Lord forgive our sins and help us to abound in His grace.

The Kiss of Peace

C:

(Repeating) May the love and peace of our Lord Jesus Christ, abide with us forever

Benediction

P:

Blessed be the beginning and end of this Holy Lent. May God aid and support all of you to obey His Will and to complete the fast. Make he clear out from before you the nets of Satan, opposing faithful Christians, and of enemies secret and public. As our Lord Jesus the Messiah at the end of His fast rebuked Satan 'Get behind me', so may He guard you and save you from all of the trials of Satan.

C:

Amen
