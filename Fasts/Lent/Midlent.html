Mid-Lent

Prayers of the Cross

Facing East

Priest:

Glory be ✠ to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Congr:

May he have mercy upon us, weak and sinful, in both worlds, now and forever, amen

P:

O Lord our Messiah, through Moses, the chief of the prophets, You raised the bronze serpent in the wilderness in the midst of the Israelite camp, as a precursor to Your death on the wooden cross. We remember Your crucifixion on the glorious cross for the salvation of our souls, the protection of the people of Your holy Church, the gift of bountiful harvests and abundant blessings, the deliverance from all difficulties and devastation, and the relief from wrath and harsh punishments, and we thank and glorify You, Your Father and the Holy Spirit.

C:

We will destroy our enemies through You and in Your name, we will trample those who hate You.

A Bronze serpent Moses raised
All those who saw it were healed
To thy Cross we raise our eyes
to be healed from all our sins
Ocean of Great Mercy, Lord
Servants raise their pleas to you

D:

Therefore, O Lord rich in mercy, we pray to you saying "Kuriyeleison",

C:

Kurielaison - Kurielaison - Kurielaison

Facing West

P:

Glory be ✠ to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

C:

May he have mercy upon us, weak and sinful, in both worlds, now and forever, amen

P:

O Lord our Messiah, cleanse us from our pride and arrogance and make us worthy to commemorate Your holy sacraments. Inscribe the life-giving sign of Your cross in our hearts. When we look to the cross, may our thoughts be sanctified and may all evil and discord be uprooted from us. Strengthened by the symbol of Your cross, enable us to stand against deceitful souls and worship and adore You, Your Father and the Holy Spirit.

C:

Do not allow us who look to You and set our hopes on You, to be disgraced.

O Saviour who has given
The vict'ry flag of the cross
Which gives life during this lent.
O Wond'rous Incarnation
Ocean of Great Mercy, Lord
Servants raise their pleas to you

D:

Therefore, O Lord rich in mercy, we pray to you saying "Kuriyeleison",

C:

Kurielaison - Kurielaison - Kurielaison

Facing North

P:

Glory be ✠ to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

C:

May he have mercy upon us, weak and sinful, in both worlds, now and forever, amen

P:

O Lord our God, of whom the prophets had foretold from ancient times and who through Moses, raised the bronze serpent in the midst of the camp, when You were raised on the cross, You urged Your creation to worship You. Lord, even though You destroyed Pharaoh and Amalek, we pray that You hide us under the shadow of Your cross. Just as You answered Hezekiah’s plea and healed him from his pain and sickness, accept our Lent and the prayers of Your weak and sinful servants. Through Your crucifixion, pardon our sins and offences.

C:

Save us from those who despise us O Lord, and reproach our enemies.

Mankind has no power to
Ward of the evil Spirit
Therefore the Lord gave to us
Fasting and the Cross of life
Ocean of Great Mercy, Lord
Servants raise their pleas to you

D:

Therefore, O Lord rich in mercy, we pray to you saying "Kuriyeleison",

C:

Kurielaison - Kurielaison - Kurielaison

Facing South

P:

Glory be ✠ to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

C:

May he have mercy upon us, weak and sinful, in both worlds, now and forever, amen

P:

O Lord our Messiah, who was exemplified through the bronze serpent, we pray to You to remove from us the deadly poison of sin, and to shield our bodies and souls from the poisonous fangs of Satan, the serpent. Just as those who gazed on the bronze serpent were healed, heal our sickness and pain as we look to You and Your cross, and enable us to always keep our desire and dependence focused on You. Acknowledging You as our Saviour and the Redeemer of our lives, we praise You, Your Father and the Holy Spirit.

C:

You gave us the shield of salvation. Your right hand upholds us, and Your guidance helps us to mature.

In the midst of Lent, the Lord
gave us the life-giving cross
to defeat evil spirits
and obtain spiritual life
Ocean of Great Mercy, Lord
Servants raise their pleas to you

D:

Therefore, O Lord rich in mercy, we pray to you saying "Kuriyeleison",

C:

Kurielaison - Kurielaison - Kurielaison

Huthama

P:

May the blessed cross of the son of God, the strong fortress of true believers, the fear of demons, and the glory of angels, guard us both night and day. Lord, redeem us and protect us all the days of our lives, keeping us from the anger of evil men, from the jealousy of devils and unbelievers, from troubling authorities, from the hands of merciless kings, from enemies both secret and public, from the nets of Satan, from evil emotions that trouble the spirit and body, and from all the powers of sin. Amen

C:

Amen

Benediction

P:

Blessed be the beginning and end of this Holy Lent. May God aid and support all of you to obey His Will and to complete the fast. Make he clear out from before you the nets of Satan, opposing faithful Christians, and of enemies secret and public. As our Lord Jesus the Messiah at the end of His fast rebuked Satan 'Get behind me', so may He guard you and save you from all of the trials of Satan.

C:

Amen
