P:

Our Father who art in heaven…

C:

Hallowed be thy name. Thy Kingdom come. Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread. Forgive our trespasses as we forgive those who trespass against us. Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord God, make us worthy to reverently worship You with the holy Seraphim, confidently glorify You with the blessed Cherubim, continually magnify You with the exalted Dominions, joyfully sing hymns with those on high, enthusiastically sing praises to You like the faithful shepherds, sincerely worship You like the learned wise men and always rejoice in You like Your holy mother Mary, now and forever more.

C:

Amen.

Psalm 51

P:

Have mercy on me, O God, according to Your unfailing love; according to Your great compassion, blot out my transgressions.

C:

Wash away all my iniquity and cleanse me from my sin.

P:

For I know my transgressions, and my sin is always before me.

C:

Against You, You only, have I sinned and done what is evil in Your sight;

P:

So You are right in Your verdict and justified when You judge.

C:

Surely I was sinful at birth, sinful from the time my mother conceived me.

P:

Yet You desired faithfulness even in the womb; You taught me wisdom in that secret place.

C:

Cleanse me with hyssop, and I will be clean; wash me, and I will be whiter than snow.

P:

Let me hear joy and gladness; let the bones You have crushed rejoice.

C:

Hide Your face from my sins and blot out all my iniquity.

P:

Create in me a pure heart, O God, and renew a steadfast spirit within me.

C:

Do not cast me from Your presence or take Your Holy Spirit from me.

P:

Restore to me the joy of Your salvation and grant me a willing spirit to sustain me.

C:

Then I will teach transgressors Your ways, so that sinners will turn back to You.

P:

Deliver me from the guilt of bloodshed O God; You who are God my Saviour, and my tongue will sing of Your righteousness.

C:

Open my lips Lord, and my mouth will declare Your praise.

P:

You do not delight in sacrifice, or I would bring it; You do not take pleasure in burnt offerings.

C:

My sacrifice O God, is a broken spirit; a broken and contrite heart, You God, will not despise.

P:

May it please You to prosper Zion, to build up the walls of Jerusalem.

C:

Then You will delight in the righteous, in burnt offerings offered whole; then bulls will be offered on Your altar. O God, You are worthy of praise. Bless us O Lord

P:

Praise be to the Father, to the Son and to the Holy Spirit.

C:

As it was in the beginning, is now and forever shall be. Amen.

Birth of the Messiah (Mashiha Dhesiledh)

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray to the Lord, and plead for His blessings and mercy.

C:

O Lord, the source of all blessings, bless us and help us.

P:

Make us worthy O Lord, to continually offer You praise, thanksgiving, glory, adoration and everlasting exaltation. Praise be to You who emerged in accordance with Your own holy will, that of Your Father and of the living and Holy Spirit. In Your grace, You took pleasure in revealing Yourself, and because of Your eternal nature, You sat unnoticeably in the lap of Your Father. Although You are above all worlds, greater than creation and invisible to the world, because of Your love for humanity, You came down and became incarnate to be with Your creation. You are the spiritual and divine One who was seen on the lap of Your mother and is the perfect and heavenly infant who blessed everything through Your perfection. At this time, when the worship on this feast of Your birth is perfected, and all the days of our lives, You are worthy of praise and thanksgiving, forever more.

C:

Amen.

Sedra

P:

In the fulfillment of time, although You created David, You were born of the daughter of David. You existed before the world and before creation, and You are the ancestor of every generation and tribe. You humbly lay in a manger, wrapped in swaddling clothes like a pauper, and You were peaceful and content in the arms of Mary, the daughter of David. You were cuddled by her and grew up like any other infant in Bethlehem. O Lord, according to the divine plan, You became man without any change in Your godly nature. It is Your greatness that made You appear in human form for our salvation, and through Your birth, You showered peace and unity, and brought reconciliation to those above and below. Those in heaven and on earth truly sing praises to Your greatness because You gave joy to both the heavenly beings and mortals, and demolished the dividing wall of enmity that had separated them. On the day of Your birth, Adam’s race received the perfect refuge. Through Your holy will, Your creation was illuminated by Your light and darkness was removed from every corner of the world. Indeed, the fiery angels proclaimed to the shepherds, “Today, in the town of David, a Saviour has been born to you; He is the Messiah, the Lord.” The shepherds praised You in amazement and sang, “Glory to God in the highest, peace on earth and goodwill to all humanity.”

O wonderful Baby, on this day of Your glorious birth through the Virgin Mary, through the prayers that we offer in Your holy presence, we plead for Your mercy. O God our Saviour, grant us a true understanding of Your amazing incarnation that took place for our sake, and enable us to constantly thank You for willingly appearing in human form for our salvation. Grant us absolution of our debts, forgiveness of sins and an enduring reprieve from all the anxieties of our body and soul.

O Lord, You are the One who makes sinners truly holy, completely sanctifies those who repent, and constantly helps and sustains all the children of Your holy Church. Remember our parents, brothers and sisters and make us worthy in every respect, to joyfully stand at Your right hand and experience the jubilation in Your kingdom. We will ascribe praise and thanksgiving to You, to Your Father and to the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

C:

Amen

Thubaik Itho (The Blessed Church)

Ethra

P:

O Lord, the blessed Fruit, holy Root and sweet Fragrance, who delighted those far and near and in every corner through Your spiritual aroma, abide in the spiritual Bethlehem of our souls. Accept these earnest prayers from the temples of our hearts. May our innermost being rejoice in the sweet aroma of Your love. Enable us to thank You sincerely, celebrate the festival of Your birth with joy, and praise You, Your Father and the Holy Spirit at all times, now and forever more.

C:

Amen
