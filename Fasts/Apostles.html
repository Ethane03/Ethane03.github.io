Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord God, merciful and full of love toward mankind, make us worthy to make memory of the Apostles, Martyrs, and Confessors, who were killed by unrighteous kings for Your name's sake. O Lord, as Your Holy Spirit dwelt in them, let Him also dwell in us, and give us tongue, power, and conscience fit to praise you and to return the enemies of the true faith to the Way of Truth.

C:

Amen.

Psalm 51

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

Merciful Lord...

P:

Let us be made worthy...

To the one Divinity and three Persons, one in Essence and Nature, triply worshipped and glorified, and singly confessed, is due praise and glory and worship at this time, on all Holy Days, Seasons, and Times, and all the days of our lives, hosho...

C:

Amen.

Sedra

P:

We appeal and pray to you O Lord, who made Your Holy Apostles the faithful patriarchs, authorities over the heavenly treasury, beloved rulers, divine servants, blessed shepherds, wise physicians, stable foundations, storehouses of the Spirit, favored by the Father, friends of the Son, and servants (bhavanakkar) of the Holy Spirit, Concerning the true father, grant us knowledge, wisdom, insight, intelligence, and perception. Let us follow you on the royal road that they walked, may we become worthy of the inheritance that they recieved, and may we unite with them to bear praise to You, Your Father, and Your Holy Spirit, Hosho...

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

Lord Messiah, the God who gave to us the priestly authority by the apostles, on this day that we make memory of them, may you accept this pure incense that we offer before you, and make us worthy to bear clean praise and thanksgiving to you.

C:

Amen
