Anaphora of Mar Dionysus

Jacob Bar Salibi, The Bishop of Amid (+AD 1171)

The Sign of Peace

Prayer of Peace

P:

O Lord God, grant us at this time love, unity and perfect peace. We bear praise and thanksgiving unto you, your only son, and your Holy Spirit, now and always and forever

C:

Amen

P:

Peace be with you all.

C:

And with your spirit

D:

By the love of our Lord God, let us all give peace among one another with the Holy and Divine kiss.

Give the Kiss of Peace to one another. Afterwards, the congregation recites with bowed heads:

C:

Make us worthy, O Lord God, of this peace all the days of our lives. O our gracious Lord and God, having received this holy and divine peace, we bow our heads in your presence.

P:

Merciful Lord, may you bless us all with your irremovable blessings. Make us worthy to do the holy will of you, your only son, and your Holy Spirit, now and always and forever.

C:

Amen

P:

O Lord, by this sacrifice that we offer to you, distance from us all evil thoughts. By it, may you enlighten our souls and purify our bodies.

C:

Amen.

D:

Beloved brothers and sisters let us attend to this Holy Qurbana which is now offered before us by the hand of the priest, with commitment, reverence, humility, purity of heart, love, true faith and devotion. To God the Father, to whom all things belong, is offered this sacrifice of grace, peace and thanksgiving in a spirit of unity and concord by the priest for us all.

C:

This Qurbana is blessing, peace, and thanksgiving

The secret prayer of the priest as he removes the Shosappa from the Casa and the Pilasa

P:

This is the flintstone from which poured streams of water for the twelve tribes of Israel. This is the stone which was placed above the tomb of our Saviour.

The First Blessing (Rushma)

P:

The love of God the Father ✠ the grace of the only begotten Son ✠ and the communion and abiding presence of the Holy Spirit ✠ be with you all, dearly beloved, forever.

C:

And with your spirit.

The Dialogue

(The priest with uplifted arms)

P:

At this time, our thoughts, our hearts, and our mind must be in the heights at the right hand of God the Father, where the Messiah dwells.

C:

Truly they are with the Lord.

P:

To sing praises and venerate the Maker of all creation.

C:

Assuredly is good and right.

Clasping his hands to his chest

P:

With fear must we give thanks to the Lord

C:

It is good and right to give thanks to the Lord with fear

The priest bows his head and prays in secret:

P:

It is good and right for us to revere and give thanks to the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, one true God

Sanctus

Raising his voice

P:

Thousands upon thousands and myriads upon myriads of firey hosts stand before you and praise you unceasingly. Make us worthy to cry aloud "Holy Holy Holy art thou, Lord God almighty", and praise you who are Trinity.

C:

Holy, Holy, Holy, Lord God Almighty
Thy praises fill heaven and earth
Hosanna in the highest!

Blessed is the one who has come
and is to come in the name of the Lord
Hosanna in the highest!

The priest bows his head and prays in secret:

Words of Institution

P:

Holy is the Father who begets and is not begotten. Holy is the Son, who is begotten and does not beget. Holy is the Holy Spirit who proceeds from the Father and is recieved through the Son. One is the true God who saved us by his blessings and mercies

Raising his voice

P:

When he prepared for the salvific passion, He took bread in His Holy hands - blessed it - sanctified it - broke it - and named it as his Holy Body. It is eternal life for those who recieve it.

C:

Amen

P:

He took the cup, mixed of wine and water - blessed it - sanctified it - and completed as His priceless blood. It is eternal life for those who recieve it.

C:

Amen

P:

He conferred this to the Holy Apostles, and declared to them: "Do this in memory of my until I come"

C:

O Lord, we remember Your death. We celebrate your resurrection. And we look forward to Your second coming. May your blessings be upon us all.

C:

Amen

Anamnesis

P:

O Lord, your Church now remembers all your salvific acts, and your terrible second coming in which you will reward each one according to their works. Therefore, we your Church and your Flock pray to you, and by you to your Father

C:

Have mercy upon us, O God the omnipotent Father. O Lord God, we praise you, we honor you, we glorify you. O Good One, we appeal to you that you might have mercy on us.

C:

Amen

P:

O Lord, we, your weak and sinful servants, have recieved your grace, and celebrate your loving-kindness for all and unto all

Epiclesis

D:

My beloved, how solemn is this moment and how terrible this hour, in which the Spirit of Holiness and Life descends in majesty from the highest heavens and abideth upon the holy mystery placed before us; and sanctifieth it. Let us all stand with fear and reverence and pray that peace may be with us and good will towards us all

C:

May peace be with us and tranquillity to all of us

C:

Have mercy upon me, O Lord, and send upon me and upon this Qurbana which has been offered Your Holy Spirit Who makes perfect all the Mysteries of your Church

P:

Answer unto me, O Lord. Answer unto me, O Lord. Answer unto me, O Lord. By Your grace, have mercy upon us.

C:

Kurie-elaison Kurie-elaison Kurie-elaison

P:

May the Holy Spirit descend and sanctify this bread that it may be the body of our Lord Jesus Christ.

C:

Amen.

P:

May the Holy Spirit descend and sanctify this cup which is prepared that it may be the blood of our Lord Jesus Christ.

C:

Amen.

P:

O Lord, sanctify the bodies and souls of those who partake these, that they be for the pardon of debts. By these may they become heirs of eternal life

C:

Amen.
