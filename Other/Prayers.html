Zodeq D'nehwe

The Mother of God

It is right to
remember the Mother of the Son of God,
who was worthy
to bear the fire that Moses saw on Sinai.
Blessed is she - who gave birth to God.
Blessed is she - who cradled the Son.
Blessed is she
who saw His face and spoke with Him, the true God,
from the true God. - Come sing praise to Him
in mem'ry of - his mother
hallelujah - who prays for us


May our vigil
be for Your pleasure and our absolution,
O Son of God.
Those who choose to honor your Mother in faith,
recieve their [pray'r] - because of your grace,
and repay them - as you have promised.
Son of Goodness,
Peacemaker of the whole world, bestow your peace
upon the Church - and on Her children.
We shall praise the - Trinity
Hallelujah - in all seasons


Who is the one,
so graceful and in whom God came down to dwell?
She bore the fire
That Moses had beheld upon Mount Sinai
ܳShe is Mary - Daughter of David
Whom Isaiah - Prophesied about
That the virgin
Would concieve and give birth to a wonder
- Come sing praise to Him
in mem'ry of - His mother
Hallelujah - who prays for us


Come, Abraham
Teach us concerning the tree that bore a lamb
Who is it that
had planted that tree there because of the lamb
No son of man - had planted that tree
And not from a - sheep was that lamb born
not by sowing
did Mary concieve the Lord who came to Her
of his own will - come let us sing praise
In memory - of the mother the Lord
Hallelujah - who prays for us

The Saints

The righteous sought
to find mercy on the day when Christ returns
And each of them
saw it with eyes of the Spirit and rejoiced
Abraham saw - on the high mountain
Moses saw at - Sinai and the cloud
The martyrs saw - as they were crucified between the robbers
they died for love. - Come sing praise to Him
in memory - of his saints
hallelujah - who pray for us

God is the light
and his blesssings are for those who walk in him
and there is no
stumbling nor harm for those who walk in it
In light prophets - saw his arrival
and apostles - believed his message
The Martyrs walked
in the way of light in the blood that they shed
Behold, that the - afflicted recieved
help from their bones - and sang praise to him who crowns
Hallelujah - them in great glory

Come now in peace,
you prophets, apostles, and blessed martyrs
who by your deaths,
have built up in true faith the church of the saints
Come now in peace - messengers of joy
Come bring us life - and deliver aid
Come now in peace - heirs of the high kingdom of the Messiah
Pray for us all - that may we enter
with you in the - bridal room
Hallelujah - and obtain life

dwells in me and makes me its slave by my will
O Good Shepherd! who went to find the
sheep that strayed from - the flock and was lost
Oh! Search for me
who am lost list the one coin, out of ten which
the woman lost, and did seek
Because you are
he who finds those who are lost
