Opening Prayer

P:

Lord God, almighty, who rules all things by your great power and divine wisdom, recieve the prayers and thanksgiving that we offer in the presence of your majesty, for this your servant, whom you delivered from the great pain and hardship of childbirth, and gave grace to come in health today to your holy house to worship your lordship and to praise your holy name. O Lord, look upon her with the abundance of your blessing, and have mercy on her. Send her blessings of richness from your side. O Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, shower your mercies upon us all.

Amen

Sedra

P:

O Lord the Messiah, who takes interest in the lives of men, who gives aid, grants good things, and remits debts, we praise your holy name in thanksgiving, for all your graces that worked with this your maidservant, and protected at the time of her labour. Now, O Lord, stretch forth thy right hand, and bless this your maidservant who has approached your holy altar. Shower blessing and mercy upon her (and upon her child). Cleanse her from all the impurity of sin. Give her grace to take hope in your mercy and to remember your right hand, which is powerful and helpful, now and forever. Hide her beneath your wings. Multiply your goodness upon her. Help her become worthy to walk in the winding path of life, to abide in true faith in you, to complete her life in holiness and to find portion in the eternal blessings of all who have pleased You. We will bear praise and thanksgiving to you, your Father, and your Holy Spirit, now and at all times, hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen

C:

Amen.
