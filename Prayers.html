Prayer When Entering the Church

O God, I have entered your house and come before your throne. O Heavenly King, forgive me of every sin that I have committed against you. O God, because you are most merciful, accept me as you accepted the prodigal son, and have mercy on me.

Pre-Qurbana Hymn

O God who accepted the lamb of Abel,
The off’ring of Noah, and that of Abraham
May you accept our prayer
And grant us our pleading

Accept prayer like the incense that Aaron sent up
Receive service like the cry of the Ninevites
As you spoke unto Jonah
answer unto your servants

Glory be to God Jesus for his grace and mercy.
For how many gifts are spread over the whole world?
When the Ninevites called on you,
You delivered them from wrath

O God enthroned in the heights by the praise of your servants
You came down and dwelt in the lap of blessed Mary
May your peace and comfort dwell
In all four corners of the world

May the prayers offered up to your pure and holy name
Be for the favor of your grace and mercy unto us
O God, font of mercy!
Have compassion by your kindness

In Your Light, we see the light, Jesus, full of light.
It’s Your Light that shines upon our world, light our minds.
May Your Light reveal the sin in our hearts.
As we humbly pray, in spirit and truth.

Pure and holy One who dwells on high, in mansions of light.
Cleanse our hearts and souls; we lift them up, unto You.
Grant us purity of thought and mind,
That our prayers may rise up to You.
