Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord, grant us the grace to stand firm in the true faith conferred to the Holy Church like a priceless gem by your Holy Apostles, and to uncleasingly recieve spiritual splendors. With them and among them, may we praise the abundance of your blessings, rejoice in the grace of your promise, and glorify and worship the splendor of your mercy.

C:

Amen.

The Heavenly King, who elected mortals to preach His true Gospel, who entrusted them with the keys to His holy treasury, who gave them authority over the deposit of His grace, who delighted them with the splendor of good gifts, who raised them up before His majesty, who by them gladdened the Church who is betrothed to Him, and caused the sheep of his field to rejoice, is worthy of praise and honor and glory on this day that we celebrate the memory of His apostles, St. Peter and St. Paul, on all holy days, times, and seasons, and all the days of our lives.
