The Service for the Transfiguration

P:

Glory be to the Father...

C:

Weak and sinful...

Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord God, of Your own will, You revealed Your glory on Mount Tabor, affirmed the faith of Your beloved disciples and showed them the great mystery of Your divinity. Instil in us, an unwavering and true faith in You, and make us worthy to praise and adore You, Your Father and the Holy Spirit. Elevate our thoughts and understanding, so that we may comprehend the glory of Your divinity, now and forever more.

C:

Amen

Afterwards, Psalm 51

Qolo

The Lord took Peter, James and John with Him...

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

O Lord of blessings...

P:

May we be made worthy...

O Lord the eternal Light, Your brilliance was ignited on Mount Tabor on a day like this, and You allowed Your apostles to witness this glorious revelation, and gaze upon the majesty of Your divine and inimitable holy countenance. We praise You because You revealed to Your holy servants, that You came of Your own will to restore Adam to his original glory, by humbling Yourself for his sake. O incomprehensible and amazing One, You revealed a glimpse of Your divine brilliance to Your apostles, and removed the darkness of ignorance from their eyes. We praise, honour and worship You at this celebration of the feast of Your glory, and all the days of our lives, forever more.

C:

Amen

Sedra

P:

O God the Word, our true Creator, because we live and walk in Your ways, we ponder on the mystery of Your wondrous salvific deeds, and continuously offer thanksgiving and everlasting praise to Your Lordship. O virtuous One, who is the source of all goodness, who can define Your power? O treasury of greatness, even the tongues of the wisest, cannot adequately praise even a small iota of Your divinity. O eternal essence of the eternal Father, You existed before the beginning of time and was begotten from the lap of the ineffable One. In the fullness of time, You came down of Your own holy will, and chose to redeem those whom You had created, because of Your love for humanity. You are the unchanging, true essence of the Father and we ascribe praises to You and sing praises and adoration to Your divinity. You took flesh of the holy Virgin Mary and performed signs, wonders and miracles. Of Your own will, You chose to reveal to Your holy apostles Your divine love for humanity, which was the reason for Your salvific passion and resurrection. Through Your divine Word You prophesied that, “There are some standing here who will not taste death before they see the Son of Man coming in His glory.” In this way, You taught the apostles whom You led up the mountain, and You showed them a glimpse of the indescribable glory and radiance of Your divinity. On this day of Your glorious feast, You took Peter, James and John with You and led them up Mount Tabor, and You were transfigured before them and Your face shone like the sun. You made it known that at the end of ages, the righteous will shine like the sun and be transformed from the perishable into the imperishable state. On this special and holy day, the clothes on Your body became whiter than snow. On this glorious day, Your true servants, Moses from among the dead and Elijah from among the living, came to You and spoke about Your life-giving passion and Peter Cephas wanted to put up tents on the mountain of Your Lordship. On this day, the Father sent a bright cloud from on high, which descended on Mount Tabor and Your divinity was revealed to the apostles by a loud voice from on high which said, “This is my Son, whom I love; with Him I am well pleased. Listen to Him!” On this day, because of the radiance of Your face, the brightness of the cloud and the voice of Your glorious Father, the apostles were terrified and fell with their faces to the ground. Because of Your love for them, You went to them, raised them and taught them that Your death on the cross would be of Your own will and not due to human weakness. We believe that the glorious expression on Your face was changed and Your divinity was revealed in Your body. Turn our hearts from vice to virtue and from the darkness of evil deeds to virtuous endeavours that comes with true faith. Remove from us the filthy covering of our impure desires and make the garments of our deeds, brighter than sunlight. Make us worthy to set out to welcome You at Your second coming. Make us worthy to be sheltered within the tents of Your glorious brightness, and to hear Your inviting voice saying, “Come and inherit the holy and glorious kingdom of heaven.” We offer praise and thanksgiving to You, to Your Father who sent You for our salvation, and to the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

O Lord our Messiah, make us worthy to observe this great and glorious festival of Your revelation, that enlightened the world with an understanding of Your purity and holiness. Empower us to sing sacred hymns of divine praises that are filled with spiritual joy and happiness. In Your incomparable grace, bring peace to every nation in the world. Enable us to ascribe praises and thanksgiving with a grateful voice, to You, to Your Father and to the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

C:

Amen
