Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord Messiah, the Bridegroom who allowed children and nursing babies to be killed by the jealous, violent, and deceitful, Herod and to become new martyrs on account of your bodily birth, remove from us deceit, jealousy, enmity, and evil and sow peace and joy among us. May we speak the truth and be far from all distress.

C:

Amen

Afterwards, Psalm 51

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

O Lord of blessings...

P:

May we be made worthy...

At the determined time, you put on flesh from the holy virgin,and you rescued and freed the whole world by your incarnation from her. Because of your bodily birth pure and sacred infants were plucked from their mothers' arms by the evil Herod without mercy and were slain by the sword, and by their murder were made inheritors of the heavenly kingdom, and friends of their Lord the bridegroom. O only son of the Father, who enacted all these things, Light of existence and most high, worthy are you of praise and honor and worship at this time, on all feast days, seasons, and times, and all the days of our lives.

Amen
