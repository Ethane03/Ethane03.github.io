The Service for the Circumcision of the Messiah

P:

Glory be to the Father...

C:

Weak and sinful...

Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord the Messiah, who was born of the Holy Virgin Mary, and took circumcision in accordaace with the Law, and followed and fulfilled the Law in all things, give us pardon of sins from your treasury, and a circumcision of our hearts to keep your holy will. Hear our prayer and grant our petition.

C:

Amen

Afterwards, Psalm 51

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

O Lord of blessings...

P:

May we be made worthy...

O Creator and Sustainer of Heaven, the Spacious Treasury, the Hope of the broken-hearted, Whose hands hold the foundations and mountains and heights of the earth, Sustinence of the poor, Who opens the granary of blessings and irrigates the earth by the dew of kindness, Who rains down many blessings, in place of tears, on those who praise him, Who blessed and arranged the months on the crown of the year by Your grace, at this time, on all feast days, ages, times, and all the days of our lives.

C:

Amen

Sedra

P:

Our Lord Messiah, Righteous, True, the True Way, Hope of those at the beginning, confort of those in the middle, and conifdence of those at the end, the eternal and unchanging light that shines on the edges of the whole earth, Who greatly glorifies in heaven those who glorify Him on earth, Who on the day of your lordly coming makes glad those who love you and observe your commandments, We beseech you that your Holy Spirit work in us, so that by your birth, circumcision, and the power of your divine name, we may attain an eternal comfort because of firm faith, a good beginning to a new year because you have defeated the old Man, and a peacful end because of your gracious care. O Lord God, let this be a blessed year, and a righteous beginning that adds to your Holy Name. this year, may all fruits and crops be blessed by your grace. grow your tranquility upon the Earth, and your peace in the Churches. May you free us from evil impurity, and cleanse us from the stains of unrighteousness. make us worthy of the tent of the saints, gather us in the houses of your mighty ones, join us to the camp of those who suffered attacks for your sake, make us an assembly selected by you, seat us to feat at the table of those who loved you, nourish us in the heavenly Kingdom, enlighten us with your true light, and organize us in your Church. We will bear praise and glory to You, your Father, and your Holy Spirit. Hasha...

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

O Lord, who took on circumcision for the fulfilment of the law, may the incence of peace that we submit before you at this time be borne up to you in the heights, may it be accepted by you as the odor of spices, and may it be for a perfect peace. Proclaim grace this year for us to rejoice perfectly and to magnify you always. Hasha...

C:

Amen
