The Service for the Circumcision of the Messiah

P:

Glory be to the Father...

C:

Weak and sinful...

Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord the Messiah, who was born of the Holy Virgin Mary, and took circumcision in accordaace with the Law, and followed and fulfilled the Law in all things, give us pardon of sins from your treasury, and a circumcision of our hearts to keep your holy will. Hear our prayer and grant our petition.

C:

Amen

Afterwards, Psalm 51

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

O Lord of blessings...

P:

May we be made worthy...

O Creator and Sustainer of Heaven, the Spacious Treasury, the Hope of the broken-hearted, Whose hands hold the foundations and mountains and heights of the earth, Sustinence of the poor, Who opens the granary of blessings and irrigates the earth by the dew of kindness, Who rains down many blessings, in place of tears, on those who praise him, Who blessed and arranged the months on the crown of the year by Your grace, at this time, on all feast days, ages, times, and all the days of our lives.

C:

Amen

Sedra

P:

Our Lord Messiah, Righteous, True, the True Way, Hope of those at the beginning, confort of those in the middle, and conifdence of those at the end, the eternal and unchanging light that shines on the edges of the whole earth, Who greatly glorifies in heaven those who glorify Him on earth, Who on the day of your lordly coming makes glad those who observe your commandments,

C:

Amen

C:

Amen
