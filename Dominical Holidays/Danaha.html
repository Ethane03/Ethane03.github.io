Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

O Lord of blessings...

P:

May we be made worthy...

You are the radiant Lord who was, and is, and will be forever. Your radiance shines through all living things, and Your glory radiates across the universe. You are the spring of all blessings, the everlasting Being, the unsurpassed Almighty, the blessed One, the King of kings, the Lord of lords, the Judge of judges and the One who is praised by those on earth and in heaven. You are worthy of praise, honour and adoration today as we commemorate Your baptism, during all feasts, at all times, in all seasons and for all the days of our lives, forever more.

C:

Amen

Sedra

P:

O Lord God who is the Maker of everything and the Creator of all things visible and invisible, You ordained a dazzling dwelling place for the heavenly hosts. When You created this world by Your holy will, You first created light, so that everything may be filled with it. You established the sky, spread it like a canopy, and decorated it with beautiful stars. You placed a ceiling above the waters and set Your chariot among the clouds. You meticulously laid the foundations of the earth over the waters, and adorned the earth with various kinds of flowers. To sustain the life of Your creation, You made the earth bear bountiful fruit, nourished the fruit and vegetation with rain and dew, and enriched the earth’s atmosphere with the flowing waters of rivers and streams. You created humans in Your image and likeness, and gave them dominion over Your creation. Humanity fell short of this great gift because of their disobedience, but You chose to restore them to Your divine nature through Your appearance at Your second birth. O Lord, who has brought us here today for this divine worship, remain in us and with us. Remove the darkness of ignorance from our souls, and fill us with the light that comes from above. Illuminate our thoughts with Your rays and make us children of light. Deliver us from every evil thought, cleanse the filth and dirt within us, and make us whiter than snow. Make us pure, and worthy of Your divine enlightenment, so that we will be able to stand courageously before Your judgment throne. Make us worthy to stand at Your glorious right hand, and be worthy of the grace and love that You have for humanity. Surround us with Your light and conform us to Your likeness. You are the source of all divine gifts, and You, along with Your Father and the living and Holy Spirit, are worthy of praise, honour and worship from all Your creation on earth and in heaven, now and forever more.

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

O Lord, You purify and sanctify everything, and came to be baptised so that we could be justified. Through the prayers that we offer in Your presence, grant atonement of sins to the whole of Your holy Church, sanctify the sheep in Your parish, and through Your holy baptism, protect everyone who has been baptised. Enable them to offer praise and thanksgiving to You with gladness and rejoicing, now and forever more.

C:

Amen
