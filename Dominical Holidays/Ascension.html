The Service for the Ascension

P:

Glory be to the Father...

C:

Weak and sinful...

Opening Prayer

P:

O Lord God, make us worthy to celebrate this great and glorious festival of Your amazing ascension with divine praises, pure voices, glorious hymns and the melodious songs of the Holy Spirit. On the wondrous day of Your appearance, make us worthy to join the angels in heaven to celebrate the festival with them, and praise and adore Your glorious and holy name, and that of Your Father and the Holy Spirit.

Qolo

P:

He who is begotten of the Father before all worlds, ascended today to heaven in Glory. He who was with the Father, goes to His right hand today in body. He who rose to the heights from below, raised the lowly race of men up to the heights with him. He who descended and touched the depths, ascended to the heights above. Today our Lord went up to Bethany with his disciples, ascended the mount called Bethsaita, blessed them, placing his holy hands on their heads, and ascended to his Fathers side in glory. The dsiciples saw this and stood amazed, and glorified the ascension of their Lord and King. Today was fulfilled the word foretold by David, the son of Jesse, through the Holy Spirit, 'God ascended with glory and the trumpet's sound'. Angels clothed in silver came down and said 'O Men of Galilee, why do you stand looking at the sky?'

C:

Amen

Afterwards, Psalm 51

Promeon

P:

Let us all pray...

C:

O Lord of blessings...

P:

May we be made worthy...

May we become worthy to offer You continuous praise, thanksgiving, glory, adoration and everlasting exaltation. You are the One true God, and You are eternally One in essence, united in will, equal in power and authority, uncreated, without beginning or end, and self-existent. You are the unchanging Light and the everlasting Power whose strength is infinite. You are the Sun that never fades, and whose beauty radiates forever. You are holy, and all holiness belongs to You. You are worshipped, praised and glorified and are worthy of tribute, honour and adoration on this day when we celebrate the day of Your ascension, at all feasts and seasons, and all the days of our lives, forever more.

C:

Amen

Sedra

P:

O Lord our Messiah, the Most High and the Almighty, You are glorious, blessed, wondrously great and beyond description. You are carried on the clouds, seated on the wings of the wind, celebrated by the cherubim in accordance with the Spirit, and receives perfect praises from the seraphim. You are the Lord God and Saviour of all. After Your resurrection from the tomb, You displayed Your divinity to Your chosen, holy disciples. You told them, “Unless I go away, the Holy Spirit will not come to You”. After You assured them in this way, You told them, “You must have divine love; love one another with God’s love.” On the holy day of Your ascension, You went with them to the Mount of Olives. As You reached and were about to ascend to heaven, Your disciples were in deep sorrow. You then commanded them, “Till I send You what my Father has promised, remain in Jerusalem.” When You ascended to heaven in great glory, the angels and archangels proclaimed victoriously and declared aloud with glorious voices, “Praise to the King of Glory, who descended and saved Adam and his descendants from the way of error and sin. Lift up your heads you gates, so that the King of Glory may enter; be lifted up, you ancient doors, blessed is the King of Glory who became the sacrifice on Golgotha. Blessed be the King whose side was pierced open with a spear, who redeemed us through His precious blood and sprinkled life on Adam’s mortal race. Blessed is the King of Glory who preached the gospel to all nations.” O Lord of all kingdoms, Your status and honour are blessed. Your coming down to earth for us without surrendering Your exalted and divine throne is blessed. Your grace which moved You to descend onto the household of the children of Adam to liberate them from the way of error, is blessed. O eternal King of kings, Your kingdom is blessed. O Jesus who is the Saviour of all, Your graciousness is also blessed. O Lord, on this day of the Feast of Ascension, we beseech You to glorify Your holy Church with divine majesty, and bless her through the hymns of the Holy Spirit. We pray that in Your mercy, You will fill her treasury with the wealth of Your good gifts, save her from dissensions, quarrels, punishments and harm, and eliminate evil from Your Church. Keep her firmly rooted on Your promises and adorn her progeny with Your support, mercy and compassion. Broaden her boundaries in all nations and guard her as she expands to all corners of the earth, so that, for as long as the world exists, she can glorify You on this day of the Feast of Your Ascension. Enable Your salvation to spread among her offspring and protect them from the evil one. In Your grace and through Your manifold blessings, lavishly grant them divine and virtuous gifts. We will offer praise and thanksgiving to You, Your Father and the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

C:

Amen

Ethra

P:

Praise be to You O God the Word, who came down from heaven for our sake, took flesh from the holy virgin, suffered and died for us, rose from the tomb on the third day to accept us into heaven, and victoriously ascended into heaven on this day. O Lord, on this glorious day of the Feast of Your Feast of our Lord’s Ascension 165 Ascension into heaven, may the prayers that we offer in Your holy presence rise up to You. As we delight in the allure of Your sweet perfume, and adorn ourselves with Your virtuous spiritual gifts, enable us to be always eager for the indwelling of the Holy Spirit that You promised to send to Your holy disciples, so that we may be worthy to offer praise, honour and worship to You, Your Father and the Holy Spirit, now and forever more.

C:

Amen
