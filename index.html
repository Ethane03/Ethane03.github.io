This site contains english versions of most of liturgies of the Mar Thoma Church. The goal is to make the treasure of the Churches traditional liturgy and hymns more available to the english speaking members of the church. In addition to the official english versions of the liturgy (North American & Singapore), english translation of the Malayalam versions are There are also translations from the 1923 Pallikramapusthakam (Book of Church Orders). These are mostly invalid now due to various liturgical reforms, but offer an interesting look into the church's past.
