Lord God, make us worthy to celebrate your living and life-giving suffering, as participants in it, and as worshippers in Spirit and in Truth. O Lord Messiah, who for our salvation was brought, on a day like this, as a criminal the place of judgement, who was slapped across the face by your servant, who died on the cross and redeemed us who were subject to sin, grant us grace that by sacred thoughts, divine deeds, and true faith, we might stand beneath your cross and kiss and worship your holy feet, obtain the remission of sins, and live peacefully and take refuge in you all the days of our lives. Sugheesa

In the tune of Hakthilu

Promeon I

P:

Let us all pray to the Lord, and beseech him for blessings and mercy.

C:

O Lord of blessings, help us and bless us.

P:

May we be made worthy to endlessly bear praise and worship, honor and adoration, and glory unfading. To our Lord Christ, the Most High, who was willingly brought to trial, who stood silently before Pontius Pilate like an ewe before the shearer, who was unrighteously condemned to death by wretched men, and who relieved us of the punishment of sin by his grace, be honor, praise and adoration, now at the hour of this prayer service, on all feasts, seasons, and hours, and all the days of our lives hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen

C:

Amen.

Sedra I

P:

Prophecies and types were fulfilled in a holy body by the Word, begotten of the Father before the ages, who put on flesh from the Virgin Mary in the fulness of time. The Mystery hidden for generations was revealed in the midst of the earth. The One praised in awe among the heavenly ranks was abused by wretched men. Though the heavens are not enough to describe His glory, they spat on His face and beat Him with a whip. The Judge of judges was put to trial today. The One who spread the heavens like a garment and established the earth over the waters, was judged guilty by the judge Pilate.

The Watchers above see him and tremble, yet the mortals below mock Him. The Cherubim in the heights bless Him, yet the wicked on earth below insult Him. The Seraphim in the heights describe His holiness, yet deceivers on earth below abuse Him. The One who is clothed with light in the heavens, was clothed with humiliation by those on earth. The One who is praised with the Father in the heavens, was counted with the criminals on earth. The One, who from the heavens, crowns all kings, was crowned on earth with thorns. The One, who in the heavens, shares heavenly glory with the Father and the Holy Spirit, was crucified between thieves on earth. The One who, from the heavens, turned bitter water into a sweet drink at Marah, was offered in return, a bitter drink on earth. The One who, in the heavens, breathed the breath of life into Adam, offered up His life on earth for Adam's race.Therefore O Lord, we pray to You with grateful hearts. We praise You because You bore suffering in your body for us. We praise You because, by Your death, You gave us life. We praise You because by Your glorious resurrection, You have raised us up into the presence of the Father. We affirm Your humanity and praise Your divinity. You are worthy of praise and honor and worship hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen

C:

Amen.

Qolo

Ethra

P:

Praise be to You, who was offered as a sacrifice on a tree. You are the One who saved Your holy Church from the pit of sin, from impure sacrifices, and from all unholy perversions. By Your blood we have been delivered from death and destruction. O Lord, because You alone are our Saviour; we revere Your cross and cling to Your passion. Accept the prayers of us who are weak, and answer unto us. Bless the offspring of Your holy Church. You are worthy of our praise and honour now and forever more.

C:

Amen.

Sedra II

P:

Praise be to you O Lord, for you who are without beginning or end, came into time and space, uniting divinity and humanity, who broke your body and poured out your Holy blood as the beginning of the New Covenant.

Behold, The Lord is in agony!
Behold, the king is flogged with a whip!
Behold, the Ruler of the world is condemned like a criminal!

Judas betrays. the Son of God bears agony in His body. Satan gloats, saying "I who defeated Adam shall never be defeated". John, with great love, follows Jesus into the courtyard of the High Priest and stands in shock. Simon denies the Lord. Thomas runs away and James hides. Annas' people are preparing the cross. A crown of thorns is being prepared. Nails are prepared. The Saviour is defeated for a brief moment. We however, have become victors. The daughter of Jerusalem who defied the Messiah is in lament. Those who put their trust in Jesus are saved. Lord, save the holy church from all persecutions and protect her posterity under Your wings. We will offer praise and thanksgiving to You, to the Father and to the Holy Spirit, hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen

C:

Amen.
