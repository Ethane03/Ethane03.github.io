Prefatory Prayers

Qauma

Opening Prayer

Glory be to You, O God. Glory be to You, O God. Glory be to You, O God. Glory be to You, True Father. Glory be to You, Eternal Son. Glory be to You, Uncontainable and Unseen Spirit. Glory be to You, Creator of All. Glory be to You, Examiner of hidden things. Glory be to You, the undefeated Power and unopposable Almighty, Glory be to You, Judge without unrighteousness, and eternal Merciful One. Glory be to You, Giver of understanding to those with knowledge, and wisdom to those with understanding. May praise and glory be born unto You from every mouth that You created and from every tongue that You made.
Amen.
