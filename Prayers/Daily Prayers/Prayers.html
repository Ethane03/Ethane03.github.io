Prayers

It is commanded that we must worship seven times (a day):
In the morning because the Lord has given us light, at the third hour because he was taken to judgement, at noon because He was hung on the cross, at the ninth hour because he died upon the cross, at evening for comfort in the night, at night for protection from impure dreams and the devil, and at midnight to be guarded from all accidents,
