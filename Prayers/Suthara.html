St. Ephrem's Memra

(Shayana Namaksaram)

Our Lord God, I pray to thee
I keep watch tonight, alone
You have commanded to me
Stand awake before thy throne

Should I fall asleep again
May the sleep of your servant
Be peaceful and without sin
Before your holy presence

If I sin while I’m awake
Or err while I am sleeping
Give to me thy holy grace
O Lord! Have mercy on me

By the peace of your great cross
May you give me blessed sleep
From nightmares that make me toss
O Lord may you rescue me

Give to me a peaceful night
With a calm and quiet sleep
May you destroy all the might
Of evil and treachery

When I go to sleep tonight
As I serve you faithfully
Send to me an angel bright
To watch over my body

From all hateful desires
Make haste, Lord, to rescue me
For I have partaken of
Your own life-giving body

When I go to sleep in peace
May you have mercy and give
Your blood to all creation
As refuge and medicine

May I reign at your right hand
In a body you have formed
May your mercies stand round me
As the walls of a fortress

When I lay down into sleep
Your strength is a good watchman
May my sleep be like incense
rising up before your throne

O Lord who nourishes me
I shall beseech you only
Let the evil one not come
aside my bed as I sleep

So that I could live with strength
You lived as a burnt off’ring
May you rebuke the devil
Who desires to destroy me

O Lord, may you bring to pass
Your promise inside of me
By your death upon the cross
May you protect my whole life

During the time that I sleep
Let me understand your love
So that at my waking hour
I might celebrate your name

I have known your holy will
So declare goodness for me
That I might follow it well
By your kindness and mercy

Declare for your servants,
Lord That they might sleep righteously
And enter the peaceful end
O our Savior Messiah!

For you are the Light of lights
And your dwelling is the Light
All the children of the Light
Worship and bow down to you

Praise to you, may your mercies
Rest upon all your servants
May it be so in this world
And in the coming world too

O my Lord, praise be to you
Praise be to you, praise to you
Thousands upon thousands more
Innum’rable praises be

O Hearer of our prayers
O Granter of petitions
Having listened to our pray’r
May you grant our petitions

Masmur(Psalm) 91

You, that sit in the shelter of the Most High, and abide in glory, in the shadow of God. Say to the Lord: 'My trust and my refuge; the God in whom I confide.' For He shall deliver you from the snare of stumbling, and from idle talk. He shall keep you under His feathers, and by His wings, you shall be covered; and His truth shall encompass you like an armor. You shall not fear from the terror by night, and from the arrow that flies by the day: And from the voice that travels in the darkness, and from the devastating wind in the noon. Thousands shall fall at your side, and ten thousands at your right side. They shall not come near to you, but with your eyes you shall see only; You shall see the revenge of the wicked. (Since you have said), 'Thou art the Lord, my trust, who hast placed Thy abode in the heights.' There shall no evil come near to you; neither shall any plague draw near to your dwelling place. For He shall give His angels command concerning you, who shall protect you in all your ways. And they shall bear you up in their hands, lest your foot stumble. You shall tread upon the adder and the basilisk; and you shall trample down the lion and the dragon. (For the Lord has said): 'Since he has sought me, I will deliver him and strengthen him;' 'Since he has known my name he shall call upon me, and I will answer him, and be with him in affliction.' I will strengthen him and honor him. With long life, will I satisfy him, and show him my salvation.

Prayer of St. Severus

O Lord who sittest in the secret place of the Most High, shelter us beneath the shadow of the wings of Thy mercy, and have compassion upon us. Thou, who hearest all things, in Thy loving kindness, hearken to the supplications of Thy servants. Grant us, O Messiah; our Savior; a peaceful evening and a sinless night, for Thou art a glorious king, and unto Thee, are our eyes lifted up. Forgive our debts and our sins; have mercy upon us, both in this world and in that to come. May Thy loving kindness shelter us O Lord, and Thy grace be upon our faces. May Thy cross protect us from the evil one and his hosts. Let Thy right hand overshadow us all the days of our lives, and Thy peace reign among us, do Thou give hope and salvation to the souls that pray to Thee. By the prayers of St. Mary, Thy Mother, and of all Thy Saints, O God, forgive us our debts, and have mercy upon us. Amen.

Nicene Creed

We believe in one true God, the Father Almighty, Maker of heaven and earth and all things visible and invisible.

We believe in one Lord Jesus Christ, the only begotten son of God, begotten of the Father before all world. Light of Light, very God of very God, begotten not made, being of one substance with the Father, by whom all things were made, Who for us men and for our salvation came down from heaven and was incarnate by the Holy Spirit of the Virgin Mary, and was made man. He was crucified also for us in the days of Pontius Pilate, suffered and died and was buried. The third day He rose again by his Father’s Holy will, ascended into heaven and sits at the right hand of the Father. He will come again with glory to judge both the living and the dead and of his Kingdom there will be no end.

We believe in the Holy Spirit, the Lord and giver of Life, who proceeds from the Father, who with the Father and the Son together is worshipped and glorified, who spoke by the prophets and apostles.

We believe in one, holy, catholic and apostolic Church; we acknowledge one baptism for the remission of sins and look forward to the resurrection of the dead and the new life of the world to come. Amen.

Litany

Pattaanngapetta deivam thampuraane

O Lord, true God, make haste to save us. From every wickedness, from every sin, from every evil thought...

O Lord, make haste to save us

From impure emotions, from abominable desires, from nightmares, and from satanic thoughts...

O Lord, make haste to save us

From every enemy, from false friends, from the trials of demons, from the schemes of evil men...

O Lord, make haste to save us

From hidden nets, from vain conversation, from great deceits, from misuse, from all the trials of this world, from everything that removes us from you...

O Lord, make haste to save us

From rods of wrath, from sudden death, from rage and from wrath, from lightning, from thunder, from earthquakes, from poverty, from war, from plauge...

O Lord, make haste to save us

From unbearable grief, from the evil hour, from oppressive authorities, from insufferable punishments...

O Lord, make haste to save us

From hellfire, from agony, from the undying worm, from the unextinguishing fire, from the grinding of teeth, from weeping, from the judgement "Depart, I know you not"...

O Lord, make haste to save us, amen

Petition

O Lord, our God, at this time of the closing of the day, we thank you and we supplicate you. Keep us this night in peace and health. Make us worthy to be found with fear and devotion in your presence. O Lord, save us from the arrow that flies by day, and the fog that travels at night.
