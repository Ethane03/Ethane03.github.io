Friday Prayers

O merciful Father, give us forgiveness of sins by your grace. Wipe away our stains with your all-cleansing hyssop. Heal our wounds so that we may praise your grace. May we who are fallen be pulled back up by you. For you raise up everyone who falls. Stretch out thy holy hands! Let us live by your mercy. Make us worthy to offer up praise to you, Your Father, and your Holy Spirit. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen.

Amen

Psalm 129

"Greatly have they oppressed me since my youth,"

let Israel now say;

"Greatly have they oppressed me since my youth,

but they have not prevailed against me."

The plowmen plowed upon my back

and made their furrows long.

The Lord, the Righteous One,

has cut the cords of the wicked.

Let them be put to shame and thrown back,

all those who are enemies of Zion.

Let them be like grass upon the housetops,

which withers before it can be plucked;

Which does not fill the hand of the reaper,

nor the bosom of him who binds the sheaves;

So that those who go by say not so much as,

"The Lord prosper you.

We wish you well in the Name of the Lord."

(Nombu Wednesday sapro orthodox modified)

As the saints of old – pleased God by fasting
Likewise, may our fast – please the gracious Lord

By the fast Moses – kept on Mount Sinai
The commandments were – given unto him

By the fast Moses – saw the Seraphim
And the Lord that they – proclaim and worship

By Elijah’s fast – his prayer was heard
And fire from above – consumed his off’ring

By fasting and pray’r – Nineveh was saved
Bless’d is he who is – diligent in them

Our Lord taught His Church – by the fast He kept
That she will conquer – Satan by the fast

Promeon

O Lord Messiah, the sun of righteousness who makes virtuous the sinners who beg for your mercy, the endless light that enlightens the world by your wisdom, at this time of prayer, and all the days of our lives, thou art worthy of worship and honor and praise. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen.

Amen

Sedra

O Lord God, you have brought us from the morning to this evening. May you give us grace to rejoice and worship at this fleeting time of evening. O our Lord Messiah, the true light and good shepherd, be benevolent to your holy church that you rescued by your victorious cross. Lead us in the way of our commands. Save those who are in various trials. O Lord, as you saved those children from the furnace of Babylon, from the sea of fire, from the terrible shore, rescue us also. O Lord, may your grace extend above the whole world. May your gifts abound. Give peace to the rulers and authorities, give unity to the priests, and forgiveness of debts to us. May the grace and mercy and presence of the trinity be among us, until the end of the world. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen.

Amen

Concluding Prayer

O thou who sits at the right hand of the Father, may you seat me at thy right hand. Place the greatness of your mercy above me. Forgive me, O thou who are rich in forgiveness, grace, and holiness. O thou who calmed the waves of the angry sea, calm the waves of my sinful emotions. O thou who art close to the brokenhearted, draw my soul close to you. O forgiver of sins and iniquities, forgive my sins and iniquities. O protector of thy own servants, guard my words and lips and heart so that I may not speak or do or think evil. May my thoughts and emotions and works and my conduct and my everything be led toward goodness. Amen
