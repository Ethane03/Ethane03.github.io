Shayana Namaskaram

(St. Ephrem’s prayer of rest)

Our Lord God, I pray to thee
I keep watch tonight, alone
You have commanded to me
Stand awake before thy throne

Should I fall asleep again
May the sleep of your servant
Be peaceful and without sin
Before your holy presence

If I sin while I’m awake
Or err while I am sleeping
Give to me thy holy grace
O Lord! Have mercy on me

By the peace of your great cross
May you give me blessed sleep
From nightmares that make me toss
O Lord may you rescue me

Give to me a peaceful night
With a calm and quiet sleep
May you destroy all the might
Of evil and treachery

When I go to sleep tonight
As I serve you faithfully
Send to me an angel bright
To watch over my body

From all hateful desires
Make haste, Lord, to rescue me
For I have partaken of
Your own life-giving body

When I go to sleep in peace
May you have mercy and give
Your blood to all creation
As refuge and medicine

May I reign at your right hand
In a body you have formed
May your mercies stand round me
As the walls of a fortress

When I lie down into sleep
Your strength is a good watchman
May my sleep be like incense
rising up before your throne

O Lord who nourishes me
I shall beseech you only
Let the evil one not come
aside my bed as I sleep

So that I could live with strength
You lived as a burnt off’ring
May you rebuke the devil
Who desires to destroy me

O Lord, may you bring to pass
Your promise inside of me
By your death upon the cross
May you protect my whole life

During the time that I sleep
Let me understand your love
So that at my waking hour
I might celebrate your name

I have known your holy will
So declare goodness for me
That I might follow it well
By your kindness and mercy

O our Savior Messiah!
Lord, declare for your servants
That they might sleep righteously
And enter the peaceful end

For you are the Light of lights
And your dwelling is the Light
All the children of the Light
Worship and bow down to you

Praise to you, may your mercies
Rest upon all your servants
May it be so in this world
And in the coming world too

O my Lord, praise be to you
Praise be to you, praise to you
Thousands upon thousands more
Innum’rable praises be

O Hearer of our prayers
O Granter of petitions
Having listened to our pray’r
May you grant our petitions

Additional verses not in current Malayalam version

Lord of heaven and angels
who is glorified by them
Worship and praise be to you
may you recieve our prayer

One in Three and Three in One
Blessed is the One True God
The Father, Son and Spirit
Praise be to you forever

Praise be to thee who receives
Like the firstfruit offerings
The petitions of mortals
and tears of the penitent

O Lord who is exalted
By the angels in heaven
Let praise be innum'rable
By the mortals on the earth

Let us life our minds above
to the triune Holy God
Father, Son, Holy Spirit
and praise Him with thanksgiving

Do not seek silver and gold
which contain mortal poison
Rather seek eternal life
and hearken to true teaching

Keep the fast for forty days
Give your bread to the hungry
Praying seven times a day
As the son of Jesse did

Moses and Elijah both
kept a fast for forty days
Our lord also kept a fast
And bested the Accuser

May the pray'rs that delivered
From the sea, pit and furnace
Open the door of mercy
for our prayer and worship
