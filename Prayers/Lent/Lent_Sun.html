Sunday Prayers

O Lord God, the corrector of hearts, reveal to us now your accusation against us. Help us to know what sins we have committed, to confess them, and to abandon them. We affirm that we have sinned. Grant us the kind of repentance worthy of your mercy and forgiveness. May you touch our hearts and soften them that the necessary remediation may enter where we have erred. Let not our sin become the reason for our destruction. We place our cares into your holy hands, O Blessed Advocate, who lives make intercession on our behalf. May your holy blood and your virtues make supplication for our sake. May your powerful intercession win for us absolution. May you say unto us “Your sins are forgiven you”, for you have taken on all the iniquity we have committed, not only for this day but of all times past. May this night be a night of rest in peaceful sleep and of comfort for our conscience. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen. Amen

Psalm 6

Rebuke me not in your wrath, O Lord, and do not chasten me in your anger.

Show kindness to me, O Lord, for I am weak;

Heal me, O Lord, for my bones are in agony.

My soul is in pain; Yet how long, O Lord?

Return to me, O Lord, and deliver my soul; Save me on account of the abundance of your mercy.

For after death no man remembers you; Who will give you thanks in Sheol?

I am weary of my groaning; Every night wash I my bed, and water my couch with my tears.

My eyes are hollowed by grief; My sight darkens on account of all my enemies.

Away from me, all workers of vanity;

for the Lord has heard my weeping.

The Lord has heard my petition; The Lord listens to my prayer

All my enemies shall be ashamed and astounded.

they shall be suddenly put to shame and be turned back.

And to you belongs the praise, O God. Barek'mor

Enyana

You humbled yourself – for our sake, O Christ
And allowed Yourself – to be tempted by Satan
Through fasting and prayer – You have taught Your Church
To conquer Satan – who fights always against her

Come in peace, O pure – and holy fasting
For you have conquered – the gluttony of Adam
Come in peace, O Fast – which Christ has fasted
For the sake of all – let us bow down and worship Him!

Sustain us, O Savior – to receive with love
And welcome with joy – this time of the holy fast
Make us worthy, Lord – to please you by it
And, Lord, by this fast – may our sins be purified

How sweet is the scent – which fasting brings forth!
Elijah fasted – and was raised up to heaven
Therefore, our Savior – gave us this armor
To raise us up to – our ancient inheritance

Lord, You are the door – and are the True Life
By which we enter – toward Your glorious Father
Lord, open Your door – and correct our paths
And make straight our ways – that we may praise your mercy

Forgive all our debts – Compassionate One,
Because of Your grace – and blot out our hatefulness
Grant us peace, O Lord – by Your great mercies and,
Lord, have mercy – upon us all in both worlds

Promeon

Glory be to the good Shepherd who by his grace came for death, and by an abundance of love entered the grave, the Lover of mankind, the Most high above all things, Invisible in His nature, the Rich One who has a treasury of many gifts, and the One who divided his own treasure and gave it unto all things. To you belongs honor and praise and worship at this time of (evening) prayer, at all feasts, times and hours, and all the days of our lives. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen

Amen

Sedra

O God our Messiah, by your saving actions and your divine love for mankind, you selected your catholic and apostolic church from every people, you purchased her by your holy blood, and made her a glorious bride. You made us your children and your inheritors. You have shone on us with the light which is the worthiness of knowing you, and freed us from slavery to death and Satan. May we observe and contemplate your divine commands, walk day and night according to your laws, and take delight in you. May we preach your life-giving gospel to our own people and to others, walk in the way that the Apostles and prophets have prepared for us, and follow in their straight footsteps. May you complete us by your will and crown us with your glory. May we show the mystery of your commands to your people and preach to them the word of joy. May we be enlightened by the light of thy glory and may we be sweetened by the water of life. May we offer up daily and beautiful songs, worthy praises, and joyful worship to you. We await your mercy, we confess your crucifixion, and we believe in your divinity. Therefore, give peace and tranquility unto your Church. On the great day of your coming let us stand on your right side with joy and gladness. We worship and praise you, your Father, and your Holy Spirit now and forever. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen

Amen

Ba'usa of Mar Jacob

O Lord our God! We cry to you, come to our aid
Hear our prayer and have mercy on thy servants

In the evening when the sun’s light begins to fade
Give us a lamp that we might praise the Creator

O Son of God, your word is a lamp to my feet
Lead me in your light which is like unto the sun

In the ev’ning, sing praise you all, as sacrifice
In love submit prayer as a burnt offering

If muteness befalls those who have tounge, mouth, and word
Still do they have minds to sing praise for creation

Into night does this ev'ning bring me, a sinner
O Lord complete this evening as my shining sun

Concluding Prayer

O my God and my Saviour, have grace on me, and forgive my sins. Wash away my stains and remit my debts. Make haste to save me from attacks of cursed devils, from the troubles of evil men, from wretched emotions, from abominable desires, from demonic thoughts, from foolish dreams, from useless conversation, from dangerous paths, from the way of destruction, from transgression by sin, from evil speech, from conversation with fools, and from everything that separates me from your love. O Lord, who rescues everyone who calls upon you with all their heart and who takes refuge in you, do not rebuke me in your righteousness and do not punish in your anger. Have mercy on me by your grace, and help me by your kindness. May you save me from every sin I have committed in your presence and incur your wrath by. O Most High, who rules everything by your grace, have mercy on me on account of your uncountable graces and make haste to save me. Amen.
