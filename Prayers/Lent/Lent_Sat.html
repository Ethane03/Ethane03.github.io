Saturday Prayers

O Lord God, omnipotent and merciful, we stand before you at this time. Therefore we pray and beseech you on account of the multitude of our iniquities. Do not judge us as you judge our crimes. Make us people who come into your presence, who call out for your mercy, and who daily experience your blessings. O God, lord and authority of all, O Lord make us worthy to live out our time living according to your holy word, and to return to you. Forever art thou worthy of praise and honor and glory. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen.

Amen

Psalm 112

Hallelujah!

Happy are they who fear the Lord

and have great delight in his commandments!

Their descendants will be mighty in the land;

the generation of the upright will be blessed.

Wealth and riches will be in their house,

and their righteousness will last for ever.

Light shines in the darkness for the upright;

the righteous are merciful and full of compassion.

It is good for them to be generous in lending

and to manage their affairs with justice.

For they will never be shaken;

the righteous will be kept in everlasting remembrance.

They will not be afraid of any evil rumors;

their heart is right;

they put their trust in the Lord.

Their heart is established and will not shrink,

until they see their desire upon their enemies.

They have given freely to the poor,

and their righteousness stands fast for ever;

they will hold up their head with honor.

The wicked will see it and be angry;

they will gnash their teeth and pine away;

the desires of the wicked will perish.

Enyana

(Nombu Thursday sapro orthodox)

Bless’d are those who fast – for at Resurrection
they shall be clothed in – glory and brightness

By the fast of Christ – He has taught His Church
To conquer Satan – and all his power

Those who please the Lord – with fasting and pray’r
Are the temples of – the Hole Spirit

The Ninevites kept – a fast with sackcloth
They repented and – were at peace with God

Upon Mount Sinai – Moses kept a fast
And in the darkness – his face shone brightly

Praise to the Father – who sent His own son
Who fasted for us - and taught us to fast

Praise to the Good One – who promised reward
For the righteous ones – and for those who fast

Promeon

Praise the one God, the creator, the one Lord, most merciful, and the God known as three holy persons. At this time of prayer, and all the days of our lives, thou art worthy of glory and honor and praise. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen.

Amen

Sedra

O Lord, the creator of heaven and earth, who opened heaven and came down to save mankind, accept the prayers and petitions that we make in your presence. Forgive us of our sins and iniquities. Bring back those who err. Make the unrighteous righteous. Have mercy on sinners. Bring those who have gone astray back to the true way. Purify the impure. []. Strengthen the weak. Comfort the brokenhearted. Raise up those who have fallen. Encourage those who are grieving. Guard those who have achieved fulfillment. Bless those who do good. Be pleased with the repentant. [] Let those who practice righteousness stand forever with the saints. Dwell with the humble. Unite the divided. Let wars cease. Bring peace to the rulers and authorities. Raise the horn of your holy church. Give peace to its children. Remove all divisions and destruction and heresy from it. Raise up shepherds who fight for it. We bring praise and worship to you, Your Father, and your holy spirit. Hosho vab’kula suban l’olmeen.

Amen

Bo’utho of Mar Jacob

O Lord our God! We cry to you, come to our aid
Hear our prayer and have mercy on all our souls

Prayer is a armor for the strong and the brave
The powerful need it in the time of trial
Thus has the Son of God has forged and polished it
and given it for battle to the King’s servants.

You who pray, be courageous without faltering
for all the pray'r of the Son of God is for you
Your prayer shall join to his powerful prayer
For by this joining shall he accept your prayer

Answer me O God, Answer me and have mercy
May you turn the minds of men toward repentance.

Bo’utho of Mar Jacob

O Lord our God! We cry to you, come to our aid
Hear our prayer and have mercy on all our souls

O Lord our God! Lord of Angels and the Watchers
Hear our prayer and pour grace out on all our souls

Father Son and Holy Spirit, shine on my heart
Give answer to the servant who kocks at your door

Brighten my eyes that they may look upon your truth
I take refuge in your kindness, Lord, protect me

I beseech you, grant in me a sanctified heart
Grant me your divine wisdom from your abundance

Help me with the gift of armour of the Spirit
Seal me in your name and guard me against evil

I love your blessing and now follow after you
I am not ashamed to ask your right hand o'er me

My only desire is to belong to You, Lord
and to worship You, O God and Father merciful

I am not ashamed to recieve Your good mercy
I shall store your gift in my heart like a treasure

Grant me life through recieving your body and blood
May your cross protect me like a strong fortress

I pray that you stretch out your right hand over me
O Good Shepherd, make me a lamb among your sheep

Glory be to Your name, O Father of the world
O Lord of Life, may my off'ring reach your presence

O acceptor of every request you hear
Hear our prayer, and have mercy, upon our souls

Concluding Prayer

O my Lord, I have placed my shelter on you, I take refuge in you. I call upon you and pray to you. I plead without ceasing for healing for my pain and remission of my debts. O my Lord, make me worthy to keep your commandments, to dwell in your court, and to receive your mercifulness. O my Lord, give me grace to live in this world a life apart from abominable things and without sin. Make me worthy of the end that peaceful men have. May you give me grace to be seen kindly before your terrible throne. May you make me worthy of the joyful experience of goodness, and the throne room of happiness. Amen
