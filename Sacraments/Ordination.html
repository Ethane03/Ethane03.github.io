Ordination of an Elder

B:

Glory be...

C:

May his mercies...

Opening Prayer

B:

Lord God, by your omnipotent right hand, by your high and powerful arm, and by your priceless holy blood, may your succor your holy church whom who redeemed. Remove vain worship from her. Grant her grace to take refuge in you, and to delight and be glad in your salvation, and to bring Christian lambs unto you.

C:

Amen

Quqlion : Psalm 15

Prayer

B:

O Lord, may you grant your divine grace to this your servants, who has come near and stands in expectation of this good gift, clothe him with the robes of priesthood, fill him with the indwelling of the Holy Spirit, and enrich him by your divine power. O Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, overshadow him with holiness from you, that he might serve your holy name with fear and devotion.

C:

Amen

Following this, Psalm 51

B:

May you be our absolver, O God, for you alone are merciful. As your recievedd the prodigal son, recieve us, and have mercy on us.

B:

O God the Father, Holy and giver of Holiness, by your grace, sanctify this your servant who is waiting to be sanctified by your gifts.
