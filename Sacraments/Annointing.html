Sedra

P:

O Lord God, You are the worthy and true Physician who is able to cure all pains, outward and inward, of body and soul, and overcome all difficulties. Lord, we pray for this Your servant who is sick (and bed-ridden) who trusts in Your mercy and waits for Your blessings and pleads through me, Your weak and sinful servant, for the remission of his/her debts, forgiveness of his/her sins, relief from his/her pains, and soundness of his/ her limbs, You are the One who strengthens the weak, purifes sinners, helps the penitent and comforts the sorrowful. You, who are the refuge of all the faithful grant remission of sins and forgiveness of trespasses of this Your servant; command his/her pains tocease and cure him/her ofhis/her illness; rebuke his/her fever and save him/her from all his/her miseries. Remove his/her weakness and strengthen him/her. Turn his/her sorrow into joy that he/she may praise You for Your goodness and help, and restore him/her to health that we may all rejoice with him/her. O God, Father, Son and Holy Spirit, who are the true Physician of body and soul, and who are good and kind and merciful, to You belong all praise, honor and majesty, now and forever.

Amen
