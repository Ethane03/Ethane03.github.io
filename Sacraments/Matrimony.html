The Matrimony Service

Introdution by the Priest

Priest:

Dearly beloved in Christ, Christian marriage is instituted by God and therefore it is holy and sacramental. You have come in the presence of God to be united in holy matrimony. The Lord has guided you until now and so you ought to remember His ways and praise His holy name. You should confess your sins with your whole heart and mind before the Lord. If you truly repent, He will forgive your sins. This service would be more blessed and meaningful, if you take part in it with that experience of forgiveness. Jesus Christ is the true bridegroom and the Church is the bride. He loved the Church and gave himself for her. We remember this ideal in the marriage service. Let us pray together for the grace of God, so that, through this married life, you may experience the love of God as revealed at the Cross even more and you may enter into a new fellowship with the Lord. May our Lord give you grace to lead an authentic Christian family life until the very last.

Hymn

This is the complete version of the hymn used in the Malankara Orthodox Church. The Mar Thoma Malayalam hymn only has stanzas 6-9, while the English version (prose) contains stanzas 1-9.

I, the Church, the holy Church
am the Bride of the Most High

I worship the bridegroom who came
and betrothed himself to me
Blessed am I, says the Church...

On the day I was bethrothed
Creation stood in wonder
I was poor and became rich
I am blessed and glorified

He made me a chamber on high
to rejoice in with my friends
Blessed am I! I shall praise
My bride groom in the chamber

He gathered me from idols
And he showed me mysteries
He told me that he would be
With me until the world's end

The King's son has given me
A promise of much fortune
From his Father's Treasure-house
Therefore, I will worship Him

Woe to the evil one who
decieved me by idolatry
Praise to the saviour Jesus
Blessed am I! and protected

He clothed me by baptism
In spiritual armor
He gave his body and blood
As a ring on my finger

The bridegroom is like unto the sun
And the bride is like unto the day
And the feast is like a tree
With tempting frangrances

When I had first heard of Him
In me burned the fire of love
even before I met him
I confessed him. Blessed am I!

I went round by land and sea
No one could tell me where he was
I searched for him in Bethlehem
They said, "He went to Egypt"

With gladness I arrived there
the kingdom of Egypt
But they told me, "He has gone
To Nazareth in Galilee"

I continued after him
and when I came to Nazareth
The people said "He has gone,
To the River Jordan"
