Confession of Faith

The candidate repeats the following after the priest, three times.

I (name), renounce you, Satan, and all your messengers, all your armies, all your worship, and all your deception.

The candidate repeats the following after the priest, three times.

I (name), confess and believe in you, Lord Messiah, and in all the teaching you have divinely conferred by the Prophets, Apostles, and the Holy Fathers.

I renounce Satan
I believe in Christ

Prayer

P:

Lord, by sending Your Holy Spirit, You have created this child out of nothing as a living being, and by Your love You have made him/her worthy of Holy Baptism. O Lord, build him/her upon the foundation of Your holy Apostles. Plant him/her to grow and flourish in your Church. Make him/her open to the mystery of the anointing of the Holy Spirit. Perfect him/her with Your divine gifts. May the hearts of these Your worshipers be kindled with Your light that they may be free from the bondage of sin.

C:

Amen

Exhortation to all

P:

Be strong, all you who, by the washing of grace, are seen in this world in the form of light. Let your hearts be encouraged. Hate the old man, who had grown weak by the pleasurable lusts of deceit, and turn your face from him. For you struggled with those works. [Now] by recieving the Word of Life among unbelievers, realize that you are quickly apporaching the new life which is coming and is promised to the faithful, and you shall be made worthy to reign with Christ according to the abundance of His great mercy.

C:

Amen

Prayer of Crowning

P:

Crown this your servant, O Lord God, with the majesty and glory of your holy and sacred name.

May his life be for the favor of your lordship and for the praise of your greatness. May he be adorned by the grace of adoption, and wear the crowns of glory and greatness, be numbered among the heavenly sheep, and make him worthy to bear praise to you, your father, and your Holy Spirit.

C:

Amen
